32 -POWER PLANT MOUNT > GENERAL INFORMATION
GENERAL INFORMATION
For engine and transmission mounts, gravity-centre support engine type has been adopted as follows: 3-point support type (two places at the engine and one place at the transmission) for 2WD and 4WD - A/T vehicles, and 4-point support type (two places at the engine, one place at the transmission and one place at the transfer) for 4WD - M/T vehicles.
  • The clearance of the stopper for front engine support insulator has been enlarged to improve vibration at the vehicle start-up.
  • Cup-type stopper cover made of heat-resistant material has been adopted to abolish the heat protector for light weighting.
  • Low-speed action spring material has been adopted for front engine support insulator and rear engine support insulator to reduce vibration transmission ratio of the power plant.

Removal steps

REMOVAL SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> SUPPORT THE MANUAL TRANSMISSION ASSEMBLY OR AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION ASSEMBLY
1.
<2WD-M/T>
  1. Place a wooden piece (45 mm wide, 200 mm long and 45 mm thick) on the transmission jack.
    noteScrew in bolts and nuts into the transmission jack as stoppers to prevent the wooden piece from sliding off.
  2. Place the transmission assembly on the transmission jack, being careful that the assembly does not touch the cooling fan.
  3. Support the transmission assembly.

2.
<2WD-A/T>
  1. Use a transmission jack to support the transmission assembly.
  2. Support the transmission assembly.

3.
<4WD-M/T>
  1. Place a 10-mm thick rubber sheet (or wooden piece) on special tool transmission jack attachment (MB991927) before attaching it to the transfer.
  2. Place the transmission assembly on special tool transmission jack attachment (MB991927).
  3. Support the transmission assembly.

4.
<4WD-A/T>
  1. Place a 10-mm thick rubber sheet (or wooden piece) on special tool transmission jack attachment (MB991927) before attaching it to the transfer.
  2. Place the transmission assembly on the special tool transmission jack attachment (MB991927).
  3. Support the transmission assembly.

REMOVAL SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> SUPPORT THE MANUAL TRANSMISSION ASSEMBLY
1. Place a 10-mm thick rubber sheet (or wooden piece) on special tool transmission jack attachment (MB991927) before attaching it to the transfer.
2. Place the transmission assembly on special tool transmission jack attachment (MB991927).
3. Support the transmission assembly.
 
 
 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT
 
 
>>A<< TRANSFER ROLL STOPPER INSTALLATION
1. Tighten the transfer roll stopper provisionally.
2. Tighten the transfer roll stopper inner and outer so that the clearance (A) between them is as shown in the illustration.
3. Check that the clearance (B) between the stoppers are the same.
noteWhen new, a transfer roll stopper consists of one part, but will separate into outer and inner part during use.This is normal.
