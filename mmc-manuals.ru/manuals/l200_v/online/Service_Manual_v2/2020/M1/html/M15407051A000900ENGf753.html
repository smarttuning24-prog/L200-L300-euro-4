Code No. C2225: Corner sensor switch
 
 
 
 
OPERATION
The clearance warning control unit sets diagnosis code No. C2225 when it receives sonar switch ON-signal for at least 1 minute, or when it detects any open circuit or short to earth in the sonar switch indicator circuit while the sonar switch indicator is deactivated.
 
 
TROUBLE JUDGEMENT
Any of the conditions below is satisfied:
  • The sonar switch signal remains ON for at least 1 minute.
  • The system detects a malfunction for at least 3 seconds when the sonar switch indicator is deactivated.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of sonar switch
  • Malfunction of clearance warning control unit
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
