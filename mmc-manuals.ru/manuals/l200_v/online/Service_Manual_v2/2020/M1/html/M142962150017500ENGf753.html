Code No.B1135 Engine switch
 
 
cautionWhen replacing the ECU, always check that the communication circuit is normal.
 
 
DIAGNOSTIC FUNCTION
When an abnormality is detected for 3 seconds or more with either of the SW1 or SW2 of engine switch, KOS&OSS-ECU sets diagnosis code No.B1135, and displays the power supply system error warning to the combination meter, and flashes the indicator of engine switch in orange.
 
 
JUDGEMENT CRITERIA
Check Conditions
  • After KOS&OSS-ECU activation
Judgement Criterion
  • 3 seconds or more have elapsed since the unmatch between the SW1 and SW2 of engine switch is detected
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Engine switch malfunction (ON/OFF seizure of SW1 or SW2)
  • Damaged wiring harness or connector (short to power supply, short to earth, or open circuit in SW1 or SW2 line)
  • Blown out fuse
  • Malfunction of KOS&OSS-ECU

noteBecause the conformity cannot be determined even when the digital tester is used to measure the voltage of SW2 line between the KOS&OSS-ECU connector and engine switch connector, be sure to use the oscilloscope (Outputs the sawtooth waveform of 0 to 5 V with intervals of 10 ms.).

Go to Step 7.
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
