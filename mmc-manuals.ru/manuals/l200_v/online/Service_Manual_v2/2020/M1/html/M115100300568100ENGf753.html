REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION <4N15>
 
 
caution
  • When removing or installing the EGR gas temperature sensor, be careful not to apply impact to the EGR gas temperature sensor.
  • Do not reuse the EGR gas temperature sensor that has been dropped.
Pre-removal and Post-installation Operation
  • Throttle Body Assembly Removal and Installation (Refer to GROUP 13A - Throttle Body Assembly )
  • Manifold Absolute Pressure Sensor Removal and Installation (Refer to GROUP 11B - Solenoid Valve and Vacuum Hose <4N15> )

2.O-ring
3.EGR gas temperature sensor
•EGR cooler (Refer to GROUP 17 - Emission Control <DIESEL>, EGR Valve and EGR Cooler <4N15> )
