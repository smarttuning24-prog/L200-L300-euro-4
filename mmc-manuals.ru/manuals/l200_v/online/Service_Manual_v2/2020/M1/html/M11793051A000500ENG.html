REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
 
 
caution
  • After urea SCR system related parts are replaced, use the M.U.T.-III to execute each command by referring to action to be taken when urea SCR system related parts are replaced (Refer to ).
  • Put the disconnected pipes and connectors in a plastic bag respectively to prevent dust from adhering to them.
  • If any AdBlue™ gets on painted surfaces, wash it off immediately with clean water.
  • If the AdBlue™ is adhered to the vehicle, wipe the area with a wet cloth.
Pre-removal Operation
  • Reduce Pressurized Urea Pressure (Refer to ).
  • Under Skid Plate and Engine Room Under Cover Front A and B Removal (Refer to GROUP 51 - Under Cover ).
Post-installation Operation
  • Under Skid Plate and Engine Room Under Cover Front A and B Installation (Refer to GROUP 51 - Under Cover ).
