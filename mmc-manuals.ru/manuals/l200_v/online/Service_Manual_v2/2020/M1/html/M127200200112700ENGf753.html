REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS
 
 
<<A>> PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY DISCONNECTION
caution
  • Suspend the propeller shaft from the body with wire, etc. to prevent it from falling.
  • Do not drop, shock, or scratch the propeller shaft.
Make the mating marks on the flange yoke of the propeller shaft and the companion flange of the differential carrier. Disconnect the propeller shaft.
 
 
 
<<B>> DIFFERENTIAL CARRIER REMOVAL
cautionUse care not to strike the companion flange.
Remove the attaching nuts. Strike the lower part of differential carrier assembly with a piece of wood several times to loosen the assembly. Remove the assembly.
 
 
 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS
 
 
>>A<< PLUG COVER/VENT PLUG INSTALLATION
1. Place the drain groove of vent plug towards vehicle wheel side and install it on the axle housing.
2. Place the plug cover slit towards the vehicle rear side and install it.
 
 
 
>>B<< PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
cautionTighten the installation bolts and nuts after removing oil and grease from threads to prevent them from loosening due to lubrication.
Align the mating marks on the propeller shaft flange yoke and the differential carrier companion flange to install the propeller shaft assembly.
