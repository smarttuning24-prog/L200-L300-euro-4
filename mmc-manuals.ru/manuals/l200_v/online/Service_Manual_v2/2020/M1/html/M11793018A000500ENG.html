Check Chart for Resistance and Continuity Between Terminals
1. Turn the ignition switch to "LOCK" (OFF) position.
2. Disconnect the DCU connector.
3.
cautionIf the terminals that should be checked are mistaken, or if connector terminals are not correctly shorted to earth, damage may be caused to the vehicle wiring, sensors, DCU and/or ohmmeter. Be careful to prevent this!
Measure the resistance and check for continuity between the terminals of the DCU harness-side connector while referring to the check chart.
note
  • When measuring resistance and checking continuity, a harness for checking contact pin pressure should be used instead of inserting a test probe.
  • Checking need not be carried out in the order given in the chart.
4. If the ohmmeter shows any deviation from the standard value, check the corresponding sensor, actuator and related electrical wiring, and the repair or replace.
5. After repair or replacement, recheck with the ohmmeter to confirm that the repair or replacement has corrected the problem.
