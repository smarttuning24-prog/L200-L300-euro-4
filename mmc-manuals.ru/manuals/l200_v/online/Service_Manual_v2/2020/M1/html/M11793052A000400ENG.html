REMOVAL SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> NO. 1 EXHAUST MUFFLER NOx SENSOR/NO. 2 EXHAUST MUFFLER NOx SENSOR REMOVAL
Use special tool oxygen sensor wrench (MB991953) to remove the No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor and No. 2 exhaust muffler NOx sensor.
 
 
 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT
 
 
>>A<< NO. 2 EXHAUST MUFFLER NOx SENSOR/NO. 1 EXHAUST MUFFLER NOx SENSOR INSTALLATION
In the same manner as removal, use special tool oxygen sensor wrench (MB991953) to tighten the No. 2 exhaust muffler NOx sensor and No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 60 ± 10 N·m
