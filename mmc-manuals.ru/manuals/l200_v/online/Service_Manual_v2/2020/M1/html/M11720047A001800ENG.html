Inspection Procedure 2: When the Clutch Pedal is Depressed, Cruise Control is not Cancelled <M/T>
 
 
 
 
OPERATION
  • Engine-ECU connector terminal CLT1 monitors the clutch switch state.
  • The clutch switch turns ON/OFF when the clutch pedal is depressed/released, and the input signal to the engine-ECU changes. According to this change, the engine-ECU judges the clutch switch status.
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
The cause is probably a malfunction of the clutch switch circuit.
 
 
POSSIBLE CAUSES
  • Damaged harness or connector.
  • Malfunction of the clutch switch.
  • Malfunction of the engine-ECU.
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. M.U.T.-III data list
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Use M.U.T.-III to check the data list (Refer to ).
  • Item No. 131: MT clutch switch.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 5
Go to Step 2
 
STEP 2. Check the harness wire between engine-ECU connector terminal CLT1 and clutch switch connector terminal, and between clutch switch connector terminal and earth
Check connector or harness wire for open/short circuit and damage.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace the connector, or repair the damaged harness wire. Then go to Step 6.
 
STEP 3. Check the clutch switch itself
  • Clutch switch connector check.
  • Check clutch switch itself (Refer to GROUP 21A - On-vehicle Service, Clutch Switch Check ).
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 4.
Replace the clutch switch (Refer to GROUP 21A - Clutch Pedal ). Then go to Step 6.
 
STEP 4. M.U.T.-III data list
Use M.U.T.-III to check the data list (Refer to ).
  • Item No. 131: MT clutch switch.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 5.
Replace the engine-ECU (Refer to GROUP 13A - Engine-ECU <4N14> or <4N15>). Then go to Step 6.
 
STEP 5. Check the symptom
When the clutch pedal is depressed, is the cruise control cancelled?
It can be assumed that this malfunction is intermittent (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Replace the engine-ECU (Refer to GROUP 13A - Engine-ECU <4N14> or <4N15>). Then go to Step 6.
 
STEP 6. Check the symptom
When the clutch pedal is depressed, is the cruise control cancelled?
The procedure is complete.
Return to Step 1.
