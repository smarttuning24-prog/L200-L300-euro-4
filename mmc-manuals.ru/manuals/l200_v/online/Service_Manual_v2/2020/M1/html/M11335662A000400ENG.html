Code No. P1308: EGR Valve Output Duty Abnormal <4N14>
 
 
 
 
OPERATION
The electric current from the engine-ECU connector terminal EGR+ and EGR- to the EGR valve (DC motor) connector terminal is controlled.
 
 
FUNCTION
  • The engine-ECU performs duty control over the EGR valve (DC motor).
  • The engine-ECU performs feedback control over the EGR valve based on the signals from the EGR position sensor.
 
 
TROUBLE JUDGEMENT
Check Condition
  • The engine coolant temperature is less than 70°C.
  • The intake air temperature is less than 30°C.
  • The outside temperature is less than 30°C.
Judgement Criterion
  • 2 seconds have passed after either of the following conditions is met.
    1. The power supply duty ratio for the EGR valve is less than 30%.
    2. The power supply duty ratio for the EGR valve is more than 90%.
 
 
FAIL-SAFE AND BACKUP FUNCTION
  • The EGR control is stopped (fully closed).
  • The DPF regeneration is prohibited.
  • The small injection quantity learning is prohibited.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Harness damage in EGR valve circuit or loose connector contact.
  • Failed EGR valve assembly.
  • Failed engine-ECU.
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. Check EGR valve (DC motor) itself
  • EGR valve connector check.
  • Check EGR valve (DC motor) itself [Refer to GROUP 17 - Emission Control <Diesel>, Exhaust Gas Recirculation Valve (DC Motor) Check ].
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 2.
Repair or replace the connector, or replace the EGR valve assembly.
 
STEP 2. Check harness between EGR valve connector terminal and engine-ECU connector terminal EGR-
Check earthing line for short to earth, open circuit or damage.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace the connector, or repair the damaged harness wire.
 
STEP 3. Check harness between EGR valve connector terminal and engine-ECU connector terminal EGR+
Check power supply line for short to earth, open circuit or damage.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 4.
Repair or replace the connector, or repair the damaged harness wire.
 
STEP 4. Diagnosis code recheck after erasing diagnosis code
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Check again if the diagnosis code is set.
(1) Use M.U.T.-III to erase the diagnosis code.
(2) Turn the ignition switch to "LOCK" (OFF) position.
(3) Turn the ignition switch to "ON" position and start the engine.
(4) Use M.U.T.-III to recheck the diagnosis code.
Is diagnosis code No. P1308 set?
Replace the engine-ECU.
Intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunctions ).
