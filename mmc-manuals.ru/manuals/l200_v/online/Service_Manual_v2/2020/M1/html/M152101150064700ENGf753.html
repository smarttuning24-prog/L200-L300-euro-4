REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
 
 
Removal steps
1.Glove box dumper <Some models>
2.Glove box
<<A>>>>A<<3.Glove box lock cylinder
4.Glove box upper cover <Vehicle with DAB tuner>

noteThe glove box inner is welded to the glove box outer. When you remove the glove box lock cylinder, you have to cut and remove the glove box inner partially.
 
 
REMOVAL SERVICE POINT

 
 
<<A>> GLOVE BOX LOCK CYLINDER REMOVAL
1. Cut and remove the shown area of the glove box inner behind the glove box lock cylinder.
2. Use a flat-tipped screwdriver to press in the lock plate in the glove box lock cylinder towards the arrow direction. Push out the glove box lock cylinder with the lock plate pressed in.
 
 

 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT
 
 
>>A<< GLOVE BOX LOCK CYLINDER INSTALLATION
Squeeze the lock plate in the glove box lock cylinder. Hold the plate and align the slit on the glove box lock cylinder with the projection in the glove box. Then push in the lock cylinder.
