DIAGNOSIS CODE CHART
caution
  • During troubleshooting, a diagnosis code associated with other systems may be set when the ignition switch is turned to the "ON" position with connector(s) disconnected. On the completion of troubleshooting, confirm all systems for diagnosis code(s). If diagnosis code(s) are set, erase them all.
  • When you replace the camera-ECU, you should teach the camera-ECU for the multi-around monitor by writing a learned value. On completion, you should check the camera image on the multi around monitor "Bird’s eye-view." If necessary, calibrate the camera (Refer to ).
  • When you remove/install or replace the camera, you should check the camera image on the multi around monitor "Bird’s eye-view." If necessary, calibrate the camera (Refer to ).
