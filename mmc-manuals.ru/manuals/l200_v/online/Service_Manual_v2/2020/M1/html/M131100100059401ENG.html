TYRE WEAR CHECK
2020 / L200/L200 SPORTERO / EU - Workshop Manual
31 -WHEEL AND TYRE > ON-VEHICLE SERVICE > TYRE WEAR CHECK
Measure the tread depth of the tyres.
Minimum limit: 1.6 mm
If the remaining tread depth is less than the minimum limit, replace the tyre.
noteWhen the tread depth of the tyres is reduced to 1.6 mm or less, wear indicators will appear.
