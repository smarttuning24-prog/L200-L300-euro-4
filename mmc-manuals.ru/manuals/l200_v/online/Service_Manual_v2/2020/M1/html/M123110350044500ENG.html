TORQUE CONVERTER STALL CHECK
2020 / L200/L200 SPORTERO / EU - Workshop Manual
23 -AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION > 23C-AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION<R6AWH, V6AWH> > ON-VEHICLE SERVICE > TORQUE CONVERTER STALL CHECK
 
 
This test measures the maximum engine speed at the time the torque converter stalls with the selector lever in the "D" or "R" position. With this test, the holding abilities of the clutch and brake that are incorporated in the transmission can be checked.
1.
warningFor safety, operators must not stand in front of and at the rear of the vehicle during this test.
Check the A/T fluid temperature.
  • A/T fluid temperature: 50 - 80°C
  • Switches for air conditioner, lamps, and others shall be OFF.
noteUse the M.U.T.-III to measure the A/T fluid temperature.
2. Raise the vehicle.
3. Pull the parking brake lever, and then fully depress the brake pedal.
4. Start the engine.
5.
caution
  • Do not maintain the throttle fully opened condition for 5 seconds or longer.
  • Perform the measurement for 3 times or more, and obtain the average value of the measurements.
  • When continuing the stall test, make sure that the A/T fluid temperature does not exceed 80°C. If the A/T fluid temperature exceeds 80°C, perform the test after running the engine at around 1,000 r/min for 1 minute or longer and then cooling the A/T fluid to 80°C or lower.
Move the selector lever to the "D" position and fully depress the accelerator pedal. Quickly read the maximum engine speed at this time.
Standard value: 2,647 ± 150 r/min
6. Move the selector lever to the "D" position, and then repeat the previous step.
Standard value: 2,647 ± 150 r/min
 
 
 
TORQUE CONVERTER STALL TEST JUDGEMENT
Stall test resultProbable cause
Both "D" and "R" position are less than the standard value.
  • Insufficient engine output
  • Malfunction of one-way clutch in torque converter
Only "D" position is higher than the standard value.
  • Line pressures is low (malfunction of the line pressure control solenoid SLT, malfunction of the primary regulator valve).
  • Malfunction of the valve body (SL1 oil pressure system)
  • Malfunction of the clutch No. 1 (C1) (slip)
  • Malfunction of the one-way clutch No. 3 (F3)
Only "R" position is higher than the standard value.
  • Line pressures is low (malfunction of the line pressure control solenoid SLT, malfunction of the primary regulator valve).
  • Malfunction of the valve body (SL4 oil pressure system)
  • Malfunction of the brake No. 1 (B1) (slip)
  • Malfunction of the brake No. 4 (B4) (slip)
Both "D" and "R" position are higher than the standard value.
  • Line pressures is low (malfunction of the line pressure control solenoid SLT, malfunction of the primary regulator valve).
  • Malfunction of the oil pump
  • Oil strainer clogged
