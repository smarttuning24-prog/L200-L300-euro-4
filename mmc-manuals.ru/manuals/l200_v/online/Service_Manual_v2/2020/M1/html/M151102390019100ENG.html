DISASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS
 
 
<<A>> DOOR MIRROR & HOLDER REMOVAL
cautionThe tab of the door mirror & holder is prone to breakage when working in cold temperatures. Always use a hair drier or the like to warm up the door mirror & holder tab and its periphery to 20°C or higher prior to works. When the door mirror & holder is heated too quickly from its cold state, it may be broken.
1. Slant the door mirror & holder upward with your hands. Then insert a flat-tipped screwdriver wrapped with protective tape between the pivot plate and door mirror & holder. Now pry off the door mirror & holder tabs and release the lower side of the door mirror & holder as shown in the illustration.
2. Release the upper side of the door mirror & holder tabs while pulling out the door mirror & holder.
3. Disconnect the connectors of the heated mirror.
 

 
 
<<B>> REAR-VIEW MIRROR COVER REMOVAL
Remove the screw connecting the rear-view mirror housing and rear-view mirror cover.
 

 
 
<<C>> DOOR MIRROR MAIN WIRING HARNESS CONNECTOR DISCONNECTION

2. Pull out all of the terminals from the connector.
