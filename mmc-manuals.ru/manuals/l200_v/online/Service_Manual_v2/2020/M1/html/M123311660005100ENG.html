DISASSEMBLY
 
 
1. TEST PLUG REMOVAL
1. Remove the 6 test plugs from the transmission case.
2. Remove the 6 O-rings from the test plug.
 

 
 
2. A/T CASE PLUG REMOVAL
1. Using a TORX wrench (T55), remove the A/T case plug from the transmission case.
Bolt size:
M14 × 1.5 × 12 mm
2. Remove the O-ring from the A/T case plug.
 

 
 
3. FLANGE YOKE ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
1.
caution
  • Before removing the flange yoke assembly, mark the position on the output shaft relative to the mark on the outer circumference of the flange yoke assembly.
  • Be careful not to damage the extension housing oil seal.
  • If the washer and sleeve are removed together with the flange yoke assembly, return them to their original positions.
Using special tool lock nut chisel (MB992700) or screwdriver and a hammer, loosen the staked part of the nut.
2. Remove the nut from the flange yoke assembly.
noteAdjust the manual valve lever shaft to the "P" position.
3. Using the following special tools, remove the flange yoke assembly from the output shaft.
  • Axle shaft puller (MB991292)
  • Drag link bushing remover and installer B (MB990842)
 

 
 
4. FLANGE YOKE OIL SEAL REMOVAL
caution
  • Tape the screwdriver tip before use.
  • Be careful not to damage the flange yoke.
Using a screwdriver, remove the oil seal from the flange yoke.
 

 
 
5. EXTENSION HOUSING REMOVAL
cautionBe careful not to damage the fitting surface of the extension housing and the transmission case.
Remove the 8 bolts and the extension housing from the transmission case.
Bolt size:
A: M10 × 1.5 × 45 mm (Seal bolt)
B: M10 × 1.5 × 40 mm (Seal bolt)
 

 
 
6. EXTENSION HOUSING OIL SEAL REMOVAL
caution
  • Tape the screwdriver tip before use.
  • Be careful not to damage the extension housing.
Using a screwdriver, remove the extension housing oil seal from the extension housing.
 

 
 
7. A/T POWER TRAIN SLEEVE AND WASHER REMOVAL
caution
  • Make sure there are the 2 washers and the 1 sleeve.
  • In some cases, there may be the washers on the flange yoke assembly.
Remove the A/T power train sleeve and the 2 washers from the output shaft.
 

 
 
8. EXTENSION HOUSING BEARING REMOVAL
cautionBe careful not to damage the extension housing.
1. Set special tool crankshaft front oil seal installer (MD998717) as shown in the illustration.
2. Press the extension housing bearing with a shop press to remove it from the extension housing.
 

 
 
9. THRUST BEARING RACE AND THRUST NEEDLE ROLLER BEARING REMOVAL
1. Using snap ring pliers, remove the snap ring from the RR planetary gear assembly.
2. Remove 2 thrust bearing races and thrust needle roller bearing from the output shaft.

1. THRUST BEARING RACE AND THRUST NEEDLE ROLLER BEARING INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the thrust needle roller bearing and the 2 thrust bearing races, and install them to the RR planetary gear assembly.
2. Using snap ring pliers, install the snap ring to the RR planetary gear assembly.
3.
cautionIf the clearance is not as specified, select an appropriate thrust bearing race.
Using a feeler gauge, measure the clearance between the snap ring and thrust bearing race.
Standard value: 0.02 - 0.12 mm
 

 
 
2. EXTENSION HOUSING OIL SEAL INSTALLATION
1.
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the extension housing oil seal.
  • Be careful not to damage the extension housing.
Apply yellow vaseline to the lip of a new extension housing oil seal.
2.
cautionPress in the oil seal to the specified depth, being careful not to tilt it.
Using special tool oil seal installer (MB992310) and a hammer, install the extension housing oil seal to the extension housing.
Standard value: 1.0 ± 0.2 mm
(From the end of the extension housing)
 

 
 
3. EXTENSION HOUSING BEARING INSTALLATION
cautionBe careful not to damage the extension housing.
1. Set special tool oil seal installer (MB992310) as shown in the illustration.
2. Press the extension housing bearing with a shop press to install it to the extension housing.
notePress the extension housing bearing until it hit the extension housing.
 

 
 
4. A/T POWER TRAIN SLEEVE AND WASHER INSTALLATION
caution
  • Make sure there are the 2 washers and the 1 sleeve.
  • After attaching the sleeve, make sure to attach the 2 washers.
Install the A/T power train sleeve and the 2 washers to the output shaft.
 

 
 
5. EXTENSION HOUSING INSTALLATION
1.
cautionBe careful to the falling of the bearing.
Remove any sealant and be careful not to get oil on the contact surfaces of the transmission case and the extension housing.
2. Clean the contact surfaces of the transmission case and the extension housing and the 8 bolt holes.
noteCompletely remove sealant and oil with white gasoline or equivalent.
3. Apply FIPG to the extension housing as shown in the illustration.
Specified sealant: MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MZ100389 (ThreeBond 1281B) or equivalent
noteSpread FIPG over the contact surface.
4.
cautionBe careful not to damage the fitting surface of the extension housing and the transmission case.
Install the extension housing to the transmission case with 8 new seal bolts.
Bolt size:
A: M10 × 1.5 × 45 mm (Seal bolt)
B: M10 × 1.5 × 40 mm (Seal bolt)
5. Tighten the 8 bolts to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 34 ± 7 N·m
 

 
 
6. FLANGE YOKE OIL SEAL INSTALLATION
1. Apply yellow vaseline to the lip of a new flange yoke oil seal.
2.
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the flange yoke oil seal.
  • Be careful not to damage the flange yoke.
  • Press in the oil seal to the specified depth, being careful not to tilt it.
Using special tool bearing installer (MB991007) and a hammer, install the flange yoke oil seal to the flange yoke.
Standard value: 0 - 0.3 mm
(From the end of the flange yoke)
 

 
 
7. FLANGE YOKE ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1.
caution
  • When reassembling, align the marks (made prior to removal of the flange yoke) on the flange yoke assembly and the output shaft assembly.
  • Be careful not to damage the extension housing oil seal.
  • Be careful not to damage the flange yoke.
Install the flange yoke assembly to the output shaft with a new nut.
noteAdjust the manual valve lever shaft to the "P" position.
2. Tighten the nut to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 126 ± 11 N·m
3. Using a chisel and hammer, securely stake the nut.
 

 
 
8. A/T CASE PLUG INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to a new O-ring, and install it to the A/T case plug.
O-ring size:
 
Inner diameter (mm)Thickness (mm)
11.681.98
2. Using a TORX wrench (T55), install the A/T case plug to the transmission case by tightening it to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 39 ± 15 N·m
Bolt size:
M14 × 1.5 × 12 mm
 

 
 
9. TEST PLUG INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to 6 new O-rings, and install them to the 6 test plugs.
O-ring size:
 
Inner diameter (mm)Thickness (mm)
6.071.78
2. Install the 6 test plugs to the transmission case by tightening to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 7.4 ± 2.4 N·m
