Code No. C2224: FCM switch
 
 
 
 
OPERATION
When the clearance warning control unit recognises that the FCM and UMS ON/OFF switch is turned on for at least 1 minute, the ECU sets diagnosis code No. C2224.
 
 
TROUBLE JUDGEMENT
The condition below is satisfied:
  • The FCM and UMS ON/OFF switch is turned on for at least 1 minute.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of CAN bus
  • Malfunction of FCM and UMS ON/OFF switch
  • Malfunction of ASTC-ECU
  • Malfunction of clearance warning control unit
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. M.U.T.-III CAN bus diagnostics
Use the M.U.T.-III to diagnose the CAN bus lines.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 2.
Repair the CAN bus line (Refer to GROUP 54C - Troubleshooting ).
