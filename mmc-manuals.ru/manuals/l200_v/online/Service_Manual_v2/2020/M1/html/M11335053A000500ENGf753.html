Code No. P0340: Camshaft Position Sensor System <4N14>
 
 
 
 
OPERATION
  • A power voltage of 5 V is applied to the camshaft position sensor power terminal from the engine-ECU connector terminal 5V and earthed to engine-ECU connector terminal SGCE from the camshaft position sensor connector terminal.
  • The sensor signal is inputted to the engine-ECU connector terminal SGC from the camshaft position sensor output terminal.
 
 
FUNCTION
The camshaft position sensor detects the top dead centre on the compression stroke of the No. 1 cylinder and inputs a pulse signal to the engine-ECU.
 
 
TROUBLE JUDGEMENT
Check Conditions
  • While engine is being cranked or is running.
  • Battery positive voltage is 6 to 16 V.
Judgement Criterion
  • The number of output pulses from the camshaft position sensor per engine rotation is 1 or less.
 
 
FAIL-SAFE AND BACKUP FUNCTION
  • The AS&G control is prohibited.
  • The small injection quantity learning is prohibited.
  • The speed limiter control is prohibited.
  • The cruise control is prohibit.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Failed camshaft position sensor.
  • Open/short circuit or harness damage in camshaft position sensor circuit or loose connector contact.
  • Failed engine-ECU.
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. Perform voltage measurement at camshaft position sensor connector
  • Disconnect connector, and measure at harness side.
  • Ignition switch: "ON" position
  • Voltage between terminal engine-ECU SGC line and body earth.
OK: 4.9 to 5.1 V
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 4.
Go to Step 2.
 
STEP 2. Check harness between camshaft position sensor connector terminal and engine-ECU connector terminal SGC
Check output line for open/short circuit and damage.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace the connector, or repair the damaged harness wire.
 
STEP 3. Diagnosis code recheck after erasing diagnosis code
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Check again if the diagnosis code is set.
(1) Use M.U.T.-III to erase the diagnosis code.
(2) Turn the ignition switch to "LOCK" (OFF) position.
(3) Turn the ignition switch to "ON" position and start the engine.
(4) Use M.U.T.-III to recheck the diagnosis code.

OK: Waveforms should be displayed on inspection procedure using an oscilloscope (Refer to ), its maximum value should be 4.8 V or more, and its minimum valve should be 0.6 V or less with no noise in waveform.

(3) Turn the ignition switch to "LOCK" (OFF) position.
