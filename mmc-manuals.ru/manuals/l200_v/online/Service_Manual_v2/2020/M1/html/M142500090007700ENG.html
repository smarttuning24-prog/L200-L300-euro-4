REAR GATE OUTSIDE HANDLE ADJUSTMENT
2020 / L200/L200 SPORTERO / EU - Workshop Manual
42 -BODY > 42A-BODY > REAR GATE > ON-VEHICLE SERVICE > REAR GATE OUTSIDE HANDLE ADJUSTMENT
1. Check that the rear gate outside handle play is within the standard value range.
Standard value (B): 0.3 - 1.2 mm (target value: 0.7 mm)
2. If it is outside the standard value range, remove the rear gate cover (Refer to ).
3. Remove the rear gate outside handle holder. Adjust the play by changing the connecting point between the rear gate handle controller rod and the rear gate outside handle.
