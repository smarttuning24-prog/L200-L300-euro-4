DISASSEMBLY AND REASSEMBLY
 
 
Disassembly steps
<<A>>>>B<<1. Bearing
>>A<<2. Dust cover
3.Inner shaft
 
 
DISASSEMBLY SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> BEARING REMOVAL
1. Bend the outside periphery of dust cover inward with a wooden hammer.
2. After special tool bearing remover (MB990560) has been installed as shown, tighten the nut of the special tool until the portion of the special tool touches the bearing outer race.
3. Press out the inner shaft from the bearing.
 
 
 
 
REASSEMBLY SERVICE POINTS
 
 
>>A<< DUST COVER INSTALLATION
Using a steel pipe, install a new dust cover onto the inner shaft.
Steel pipemm
Overall length50
Outside diameter75
Wall thickness4
 

 
 
>>B<< BEARING INSTALLATION
Use special tool bearing remover (MB990560) to press-fit the bearing onto the inner shaft.
