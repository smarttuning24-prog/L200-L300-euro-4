Continuity Check at Wiring Harness-side Connector
1. Turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position.
2. Disconnect the ASTC-ECU connector.
3. Check the resistance and continuity between the terminals indicated in the table below.
4. The terminal layout is shown in the illustration.
Terminal No.SignalNormal condition
13 to body earthEarthContinuity exists (2 Ω or less)
38 to body earthEarthContinuity exists (2 Ω or less)
