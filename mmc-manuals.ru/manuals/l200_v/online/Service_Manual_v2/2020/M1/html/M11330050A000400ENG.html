ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE SENSOR CHECK
2020 / L200/L200 SPORTERO / EU - Workshop Manual
13 -FUEL > 13A-DIESEL FUEL > ON-VEHICLE SERVICE > ENGINE COOLANT TEMPERATURE SENSOR CHECK
1.
cautionDo not tilt the special tool engine coolant temperature sensor wrench (MB992042) as the engine coolant temperature sensor connector may be damaged.
Remove the engine coolant temperature sensor using the special tool engine coolant temperature sensor wrench (MB992042).
2. With temperature sensing portion of engine coolant temperature sensor immersed in hot water, check resistance.
Standard value:
  • 14 to 17 kΩ (at -20°C)
  • [5.1 to 6.5 kΩ (at 0°C)]
  • 2.1 to 2.7 kΩ (at 20°C)
  • [0.9 to 1.3 kΩ (at 40°C)]
  • [0.48 to 0.68 kΩ (at 60°C)]
  • [0.26 to 0.36 kΩ (at 80°C)]
note[ ]: Reference value.
3. If the resistance deviates from the standard value greatly, replace the sensor.
4. Apply the sealant over the screw sections on the engine coolant temperature sensor.
Specified sealant:
  • ThreeBond 1324N or equivalent
5. Tighten the engine coolant temperature sensor to the specified torque, using special tool engine coolant temperature sensor wrench (MB992042).
Tightening torque: 30 ± 9 N·m
