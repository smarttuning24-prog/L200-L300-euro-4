Code No.B1130 Ignition power supply
 
 
caution
  • If diagnosis code No. B1130 is set, always diagnose the CAN bus lines.
  • Before replacing ECU, ensure that the communication circuit is normal.
 
 
DIAGNOSTIC FUNCTION
If the output power supply status is different from the engine switch status information received from ETACS-ECU via CAN, KOS&OSS-ECU sets diagnosis code No.B1130. At the same time, KOS&OSS-ECU displays the power supply system error warning to the combination meter, and flashes the indicator of engine switch in orange.
 
 
JUDGEMENT CRITERIA
Check Conditions
  • The terminal voltage of IOD and +B is between 10 and 16 V.
Judgement Criterion
  • Any of the following conditions is met, and 2 seconds or more have elapsed.
    1. The engine switch status information received from ETACS-ECU is "ON" when the power supply mode is at OFF.
    2. The engine switch status information received from ETACS-ECU is "ON" when the power supply mode is at ACC.
    3. The engine switch status information received from ETACS-ECU is "ON" when the power supply mode is at ON.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of the CAN bus line
  • Damaged wiring harness or connector (short to earth or short to power supply or open circuit in IG1 line)
  • Malfunction of IG1 relay
  • Malfunction of KOS&OSS-ECU
  • Malfunction of ETACS-ECU
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. M.U.T.-III CAN bus diagnostics
After performing the forced power supply output (Refer to ) IG-ON using the M.U.T.-III, diagnose the CAN bus line.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 2.
Repair the CAN bus line (Refer to GROUP 54C - Troubleshooting ).
 
STEP 2. M.U.T.-III diagnosis code
Check if KOS&OSS-ECU diagnosis code No.B1133 is set.
Is the diagnosis code set?
Troubleshoot for the relevant diagnosis code (Refer to ).
Go to Step 3.
 
STEP 3. IG1 relay check
Check that the IG1 relay works normally (Refer to ).
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 4.
Replace the IG1 relay.
 
STEP 4. Voltage measurement at IG1 relay connector (IG1 line)
(1) Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2) Turn the engine switch from "LOCK" (OFF) position to "ON" position.
(3) Measure the voltage between the IG1 relay connector (IG1 line) and the body earth.
OK: System voltage
(4) Turn the engine switch from "ON" position to "LOCK" (OFF) position.
(5) Measure the voltage between the IG1 relay connector (IG1 line) and the body earth.
OK: 1 V or less
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 6.
Go to Step 5.
 
STEP 5. Check of short to power supply, short to earth or open circuit in IG1 line between the KOS&OSS-ECU connector and the IG1 relay connector
Is the check result normal?
Intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 6. Check of open circuit in earth line between the IG1 relay connector and the body earth
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 7.
Repair the wiring harness.
 
STEP 7. Voltage measurement at IG1 relay connector (IG1 line)
(1) Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2) Measure the voltage between the IG1 relay connector (power supply line) and the fusible link connector.
OK: System voltage
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 9.
Go to Step 8.
 
STEP 8. Check of short to earth or open circuit in power supply line between the fusible link connector and the IG1 relay connector
Is the check result normal?
Intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 9. Voltage measurement at ETACS-ECU connector (IG1 line)
(1) Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2) Turn the engine switch from "LOCK" (OFF) position to "ON" position.
(3) Measure the voltage between the ETACS-ECU connector (IG1 line) and the body earth.
OK: System voltage
(4) Turn the engine switch from "ON" position to "LOCK" (OFF) position.
(5) Measure the voltage between the ETACS-ECU connector (IG1 line) and the body earth.
OK: 1 V or less
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 10.
Repair the wiring harness, IG1 line between the ETACS-ECU connector and the fusible link.
 
STEP 10. M.U.T.-III other system data list
Operate the engine switch to change the power supply mode, and check that the data list display of ETACS-ECU is changed.
  • When power supply mode is "OFF".
 
ETACS-ECU data list
Item No.Item nameNormal condition
30Ignition switch(IG1)OFF
  • When power supply mode is "ON".
 
ETACS-ECU data list
Item No.Item nameNormal condition
30Ignition switch(IG1)ON
OK: Normal conditions are displayed for the items.
Are the check result normal?
YES : Go to Step 11.
Go to Step 12.
 
STEP 11. Check whether the diagnosis code is set again.
Check again if the diagnosis code is set in the KOS&OSS-ECU.
(1) Erase the diagnosis code.
(2) Turn the engine switch from "LOCK" (OFF) position to "ON" position.
(3) On completion, check that the diagnosis code is set again.
Is the diagnosis code set?
Replace the ETACS-ECU (Refer to GROUP 54A - ETACS-ECU ).
Intermittent malfunction is suspected (Refer to GROUP 00, How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunctions ).
 
STEP 12. Check whether the diagnosis code is set again.
Check again if the diagnosis code is set in the KOS&OSS-ECU.
(1) Erase the diagnosis code.
(2) Turn the engine switch from "LOCK" (OFF) position to "ON" position.
(3) On completion, check that the diagnosis code is not set again.
Is the diagnosis code set?
Replace the KOS&OSS-ECU and register the chassis number (Refer to ).
Intermittent malfunction is suspected (Refer to GROUP 00, How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunctions ).
