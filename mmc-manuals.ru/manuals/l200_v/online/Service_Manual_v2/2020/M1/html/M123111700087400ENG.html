REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS
 
 
<<A>> STARTER MOUNTING BOLT REMOVAL
Remove the starter with its connector connected. Keep the starter fixed to the engine side (Refer to GROUP 16 - Starter Motor Assembly).
 
 
 
<<B>> SUPPORTING THE ENGINE
cautionSupport the engine assembly by inserting a wooden piece between the front suspension front crossmember assembly and the engine oil pan to prevent the engine assembly from being tilted significantly when the transmission assembly is separated from the engine assembly.
 
 

 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS
 
 
>>A<< TRANSMISSION ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
Fully push the torque converter into the transmission side, and then assemble the transmission assembly to the engine.
 

 
 
>>B<< A/T CASE PROTECTOR INSTALLATION
Temporarily tighten each bolt shown in the illustration, then tighten the bolts to the specified torque in the order of A and B to install the A/T case protector to the transmission case.
Tightening torque:
  • 18 ± 3 N·m (A)
  • 11 ± 1 N·m (B)
