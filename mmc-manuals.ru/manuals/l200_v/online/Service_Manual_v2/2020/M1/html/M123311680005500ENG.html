DISASSEMBLY
 
 
1. TEST PLUG REMOVAL
1. Remove the 6 test plugs from the transmission case.
2. Remove the 6 O-rings from the test plug.
 

 
 
2. A/T CASE PLUG REMOVAL
1. Using a TORX wrench (T55), remove the A/T case plug from the transmission case.
Bolt size:
M14 × 1.5 × 12 mm
2. Remove the O-ring from the A/T case plug.
 

 
 
3. TUBE REMOVAL
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the case or adapter.
  • Be careful not to bend the tube.
  • Never reuse the removed tube.
Remove the 2 tubes from the transmission case adapter.
 

 
 
4. TRANSMISSION CASE ADAPTER REMOVAL
1. Remove the 10 bolts and the transmission case adapter.
Bolt size:
M10 × 1.5 × 50 mm (Seal bolt)
2.
cautionBe careful not to damage the fitting surface of the transmission case adapter and the transmission case.
Using a plastic hammer, tap the transmission case adapter to remove it.
 

 
 
5. TRANSMISSION CASE ADAPTER BEARING REMOVAL
1.
cautionBe careful not to damage the transmission case adapter.
Using a screwdriver, remove the snap ring from the transmission case adapter.
2.
cautionBe careful not to damage the transmission case adapter.
Set special tool installer (MB992216) as shown in the illustration, and press the transmission case adapter bearing with a shop press to remove it from the transmission case adapter.
 

 
 
6. TRANSMISSION CASE ADAPTER OIL SEAL REMOVAL
caution
  • Tape the screwdriver tip before use.
  • Be careful not to damage the transmission case adapter.
  • If using a screwdriver, use a wooden block or equivalent to avoid damaging the fitting surface of the transmission case adapter.
Using a screwdriver, remove the transmission case adapter oil seal from the transmission case adapter.
 
 
 
 
REASSEMBLY
 
 
1. TRANSMISSION CASE ADAPTER BEARING INSTALLATION
1.
cautionBe careful not to damage the transmission case adapter.
Set special tool oil seal installer (MB992312) as shown in the illustration, and press a new transmission case adapter bearing with a shop press to install it to the transmission case adapter.
notePress the transmission case adapter bearing until it hit the transmission case adapter.
2.
cautionBe careful not to damage the transmission case adapter.
Using a screwdriver, install the snap ring to the transmission case adapter.
 

 
 
2. TRANSMISSION CASE ADAPTER OIL SEAL INSTALLATION
1. Apply yellow vaseline to the lip of a new transmission case adapter oil seal.
2.
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the transmission case adapter oil seal.
  • Be careful not to damage the transmission case adapter.
  • Press in the oil seal to the specified depth, being careful not to tilt it.
Using special tool differential oil seal installer (MB990699) and a hammer, install the transmission case adapter oil seal to the transmission case adapter.
Standard value: 0 - 0.5 mm
(From the end of the transmission case adapter)
 

 
 
3. TRANSMISSION CASE ADAPTER INSTALLATION
1. Remove any sealant and be careful not to get oil on the contact surfaces of the transmission case and the transmission case adapter.
2. Clean the contact surfaces of the transmission case and the transmission case adapter and the 10 bolt holes.
noteCompletely remove sealant and oil with white gasoline or equivalent.
3. Apply FIPG to the transmission case adapter as shown in the illustration.
Specified sealant: MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MZ100389 (ThreeBond 1281B) or equivalent
noteSpread FIPG over the contact surface.
4.
cautionBe careful not to damage the fitting surface of the transmission case adapter and the transmission case.
Install the transmission case adapter with 10 new seal bolts to the transmission case.
Bolt size:
M10 × 1.5 × 50 mm (Seal bolt)
5. Tighten the 10 bolts to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 34 ± 7 N·m
 

 
 
4. TUBE INSTALLATION
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the case or adapter.
  • Be careful not to bend or break the tube.
  • Be sure to use a new tube.
Install 2 new tubes to the transmission case adapter.
 

 
 
5. A/T CASE PLUG INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to a new O-ring, and install it to the A/T case plug.
Bolt size:
M14 × 1.5 × 12 mm
O-ring size:
 
Inner diameter (mm)Thickness (mm)
11.681.98
2. Using a TORX wrench (T55), install the A/T case plug to the transmission case by tightening it to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 39 ± 15 N·m
 

 
 
6. TEST PLUG INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to 6 new O-rings, and install them to the 6 test plugs.
O-ring size:
 
Inner diameter (mm)Thickness (mm)
6.071.78
2. Install the 6 test plugs to the transmission case by tightening to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 7.4 ± 2.4 N·m
