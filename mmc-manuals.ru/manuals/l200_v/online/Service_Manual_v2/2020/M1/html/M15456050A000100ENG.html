Inspection Procedure 4: The System does not Enter the Test Mode When the Emergency Call Switch is Operated.
 
 
cautionWhen replacing the ECU, always check that the communication circuit is normal.
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
If the system does not enter the test mode, the emergency call switch circuit is opened, the emergency call switch or the emergency call control unit may fail.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Damaged wiring harness or connector(s)
  • Malfunction of emergency call switch
  • Malfunction of emergency call control unit
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. Check emergency call switch.
Refer to .
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 2.
Replace the emergency call switch (Refer to ).
 
STEP 2. Resistance measurement at between the emergency call control unit connector and the emergency call switch connector
(1) Disconnect the emergency call control unit connector and the emergency call switch connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2) Check the resistance between the emergency call control unit connector terminal SW1+ and the emergency call switch connector terminal No. 8.
(3) Check the resistance between the emergency call control unit connector terminal SW1- and the emergency call switch connector terminal No. 14.
OK: Continuity exists (2 Ω or less)
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 3.
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 3. Retest the system.
Is the check result normal?
The diagnosis is complete.
Replace the emergency call control unit (Refer to ).
