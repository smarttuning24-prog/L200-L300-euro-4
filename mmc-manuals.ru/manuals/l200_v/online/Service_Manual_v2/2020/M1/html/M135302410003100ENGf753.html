Inspection Procedure 18: The HDC Does not Work. <Vehicles with HDC Function>
 
 
caution
  • If there is any problem in the CAN bus lines, an incorrect diagnosis code may be set. Prior to this diagnosis, diagnose the CAN bus lines (Refer to GROUP 54C - CAN Bus Diagnostics Table ).
  • Whenever ECU is replaced, ensure that the CAN bus lines are normal.
  • When the hydraulic unit (integrated with ASTC-ECU) is replaced, always carry out the calibration of the steering wheel sensor and the G and yaw rate sensor (Refer to ).
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
The HDC is not activated under any of the following conditions:
  • The HDC is turned OFF
  • The vehicle is stationary or the vehicle speed is 20 km/h or more
  • On a gentle gradient
  • Rear differential lock system: Lock
  • Gearshift lever: Forward gear except the 1st position
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Damaged wiring harness or connector
  • Malfunction of the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU)
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. M.U.T.-III CAN bus diagnosis
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Use the M.U.T.-III to diagnose the CAN bus lines.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 3.
Repair the CAN bus lines (Refer to GROUP 54C - CAN Bus Diagnostics Table ). Then go to Step 2.
 
STEP 2. Retest the system
Is the HDC activated when you drive downhill on a non-gentle gradient at 20 km/h or less with the HDC turned ON?
Is the check result normal?
This diagnosis is complete.
Go to Step 3.
 
STEP 3. M.U.T.-III diagnosis code
Use M.U.T.-III to check the diagnosis code .
Is any diagnosis code set?
Perform the troubleshooting for the diagnosis code (Refer to Diagnosis Code Chart ). Then go to Step 7.
Go to Step 4.
 
STEP 4. Operate the HDC switch to check the system
Is the HDC turned ON when you operate the HDC switch while all of the following conditions are satisfied?
  • Stop the vehicle.
  • Rear differential lock system: Unlock
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 5. <M/T>
Go to Step 6. <A/T>
Perform the symptom procedure (Refer to Inspection Procedure 19: The HDC is not turned ON or OFF by operating the HDC switch ). Then go to Step 7 .
 
STEP 5. Check the HDC function <M/T>
Is the HDC activated when the gearshift lever is moved to the 1st position?
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 6.
1st position switch circuit inspection (Refer to Diagnosis Code Procedures, Code No. C1296: Malfunction of 1st gear switch circuit ). Then go to Step 7 .
 
STEP 6. Retest the system
Is the HDC activated when you drive downhill on a non-gentle gradient at 20 km/h or less with the HDC turned ON?
Is the check result normal?
The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Replace the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) (Refer to ). Then go to Step 7 .
 
STEP 7. Retest the system
Is the HDC activated when you drive downhill on a non-gentle gradient at 20 km/h or less with the HDC turned ON?
Is the check result normal?
This diagnosis is complete.
Return to Step 1.
