Code No. P220E: No. 1 NOx Sensor Heater Control Circuit Range/Performance
 
 
 
 
FUNCTION
The DCU sets diagnosis code No. P220E when the heater temperature received from the No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor via CAN communication does not rise.
 
 
TROUBLE JUDGEMENT
Check Conditions
  • After the No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor heater is activated.
  • Engine is running.
Judgement Criterion
  • The temperature of the No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor heater does not rise for 120 seconds or more.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
Failed No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
Replace the No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor (Refer to ).
