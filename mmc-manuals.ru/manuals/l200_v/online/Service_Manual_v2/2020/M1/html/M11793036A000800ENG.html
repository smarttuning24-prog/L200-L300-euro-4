UREA INJECTOR ASSEMBLY CHECK
2020 / L200/L200 SPORTERO / EU - Workshop Manual
17 -ENGINE AND EMISSION CONTROL > EMISSION CONTROL <DIESEL> > UREA SCR SYSTEM<4N14> > UREA INJECTOR CHECK
 
 
Measurement of Resistance Between Terminals
1.
cautionPut the disconnected connector in a plastic bag respectively to prevent dust from adhering to them.
Disconnect the urea injector assembly connector.
2. Measure the resistance between terminals.
Standard value: 11.4 to 12.6 Ω (at 20°C)
3. Measure the resistance between urea injector body and either terminal No. 1 or terminal No. 2 of the connector at the urea injector assembly (check short to earth in the internal circuit of the urea injector).
Standard value: No continuity
4. If the resistance is deviates from the standard value, replace the urea injector assembly.
5. Connect the urea injector assembly connector.
 
 
 
Check The Urea Pressure
1.
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Connect the M.U.T.-III to the diagnosis connector.
2. Warm up the engine until data list item No.15: Supply pump power stage status is "ON".
3. Check the data list item No. 6: SCR sub state machine and item No. 9: Modeled urea pressure.
Standard value: SCR sub state machine reaches to "Metering control", and Modeled urea pressure reaches to "5,000 to 6,500 hPa".
4. After turning the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position, remove the M.U.T.-III.
 
 
 
Check The Urea Injector Assembly Condition
1. Remove the urea injector assembly from the catalytic converter without disconnecting the urea injector assembly connector and the urea line tube A from the urea injector assembly (Refer to ).
2.
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Connect the M.U.T.-III to the diagnosis connector.
3. Turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position (but do not start the engine).
4. Select "DCU" from System Select Screen of the M.U.T.-III.
5. From DCU Screen, select "Special Function".
6. From Special Function Screen, select "Test".
7. Carry out the "Item No. 3: Urea pumping and injection test" from Test Screen of the M.U.T.-III.
noteIt takes approximately 30 seconds until the AdBlue™ is injected from the urea injector assembly after the urea pumping and injection test is performed because it takes time until the urea line tube is filled with the AdBlue™.
8.
cautionIf the AdBlue™ is adhered to the vehicle, wipe the area with a wet cloth.
Check the injection status of the urea injector assembly.
Standard value: The AdBlue™ is injected from each injection port of the urea injector assembly equally.
9. Stop "Item No. 3: Urea pumping and injection test".
10. If AdBlue™ is not equally injected from each injection port of the urea injector assembly, replace the urea injector assembly.
11. After turning the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position, remove the M.U.T.-III.
12. Install the urea injector assembly in the original places.
 
 
 
Check The Urea Leaks
1. Remove the urea injector assembly from the catalytic converter without disconnecting the urea injector assembly connector and the urea line tube A from the urea injector assembly (Refer to ).
2.
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Connect the M.U.T.-III to the diagnosis connector.
3. Turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position (but do not start the engine).
4. Select "DCU" from System Select Screen of the M.U.T.-III.
5. From DCU Screen, select "Special Function".
6. From Special Function Screen, select "Test".
7. Carry out the "Item No. 2: Urea leak test" from Test Screen of the M.U.T.-III.
8.
cautionIf the AdBlue™ is adhered to the vehicle, wipe the area with a wet cloth.
Check for leakage from the urea injector nozzle and urea line tube.
Normal condition: During the urea leak test, the AdBlue™ does not leak from the urea injector nozzle or urea line tube.
9. If AdBlue™ is leaking from each injection nozzle or urea line tube, replace the urea injector assembly or urea line tube.
10. After turning the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position, remove the M.U.T.-III.
11. Install the urea injector assembly in the original places.
