46Idle stop enable (transfer-ECU)
[Idle Stop enable(T/F ECU)]
When auto stop prohibition signal is not received from transfer-ECUEnable
When auto stop prohibition signal is received from transfer-ECU*8Disable
note
  • *1: If the DC/DC converter body or the DC/DC converter circuit is abnormal, "OFF" or "Failure" should be shown.
  • *2: If the combination meter detects the seat belt malfunction, "Fault" should be shown. If the communication error occurs between the ETACS-ECU and the combination meter, "SNA" should be shown.
  • *3: If the engine control system is abnormal, "ON" should be shown.
  • *4: The conditions for changing the vehicle status to the auto stop status are as shown below:
    • More than 3 minutes have passed since the engine was started
    • AS&G system: ON
    • Hood: Close
    • Fasten the driver’s seat belt
    • Lamps and all accessories: OFF
    • Engine: After warm-up
    • Ambient temperature: more than 0°C
    • AS&G system is normal
    • Stop the vehicle after driving at 5 km/h or more. Then place the gearshift lever in the neutral position. <M/T>
    • Stop the vehicle after driving at 5 km/h or more. Then place the selector lever in the "D" position. <A/T>
    • Clutch pedal: Released <M/T>
    • Brake pedal: Depress <A/T>
  • *5: If the AS&G system is abnormal, "Turn on" and "Turn off" should repeatedly and alternately stay on the display.
  • *6: If the A/T system is abnormal, "ON" should be shown.
  • *7: If the ASTC system is abnormal, "ON" should be shown.
  • *8: See the conditions of the auto stop prohibition signal for the transfer-ECU. <4WD>
