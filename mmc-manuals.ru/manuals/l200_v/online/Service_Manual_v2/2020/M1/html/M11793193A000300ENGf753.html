Inspection Procedure 3: Diagnose Shorts in The Earth to ENG-LOCAL CAN.
 
 
cautionWhen servicing a CAN bus line, earth yourself by touching a metal object such as an unpainted water pipe. If you fail to do, a component connected to the CAN bus line may be broken.
 
 
TROUBLE JUDGEMENT CONDITIONS
The M.U.T.-III judges the trouble when it is impossible to receive the periodically sent data and the voltage of CAN_H or CAN_L line is less than 1.0 V.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of the wiring harness
  • Malfunction of the connector
  • Malfunction in the ECUs or sensors which are interconnected via ENG-LOCAL CAN (internal error) (DCU, Engine-ECU, No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor, No. 2 exhaust muffler NOx sensor, exhaust muffler PM sensor)
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. Check the ENG-LOCAL CAN. Resistance measurement at DCU connector.
caution
  • A digital multimeter should be used (For details refer to GROUP 54C - Service Precautions ).
  • The test wiring harness should be used (For details refer to GROUP 54C - Service Precautions ).
  • When measuring the resistance, disconnect the negative battery terminal (For details refer to GROUP 54C - Service Precautions ).
noteThis inspection allows you to check that there is a short to earth in either CAN_H line or CAN_L line. Thus, in the following steps, check the ENG-LOCAL CAN that is defective.
(1) Ensure that the negative battery terminal is disconnected.
(2) Disconnect engine-ECU connector and DCU connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(3) Resistance between DCU connector terminal CAN_H and body earth.
OK: 1 kΩ or more
(4) Resistance between DCU connector terminal CAN_L and body earth.
OK: 1 kΩ or more
Is the check result normal?
YES <Resistance value is 1 kΩ or more in both CAN_H and CAN_L> : Go to Step 13.
NO <Resistance value is less than 1 kΩ in either CAN_H or CAN_L> : Go to Step 2.
 
STEP 2. Check the ENG-LOCAL CAN between joint connector (CAN4) and the DCU. Resistance measurement at joint connector (CAN4).
caution

  • Strictly observe the specified wiring harness repair procedure (For details refer to GROUP 54C - Service Precautions ).

OK: 1 kΩ or more
Is the check result normal?

NO <Resistance value is less than 1 kΩ in either CAN_H or CAN_L> : Repair the wiring harness between joint connector (CAN4) and the DCU connector.

STEP 4. Diagnose the ENG-LOCAL CAN (No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor connector disconnected)
cautionStrictly observe the specified wiring harness repair procedure (For details refer to GROUP 54C - Service Precautions ).
(1) Disconnect No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor connector and read all diagnosis with M.U.T.-III.
(2) Confirm that all diagnosis reading result of the DCU is NC.
Is all diagnosis reading result of the DCU NC?
Repair the wiring harness between joint connector (CAN4) and the No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor connector.
Check the No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor connector, and repair if necessary. If the No. 1 exhaust muffler NOx sensor connector is in good condition, go to Step 5.
