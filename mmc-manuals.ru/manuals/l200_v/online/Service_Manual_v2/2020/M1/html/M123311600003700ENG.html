DISASSEMBLY
 
 
1. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 1 (B1) REMOVAL
1.
cautionBe careful not to damage the transmission case.
Using a screwdriver, remove the snap ring from the transmission case.
2. Remove the flange, the 4 discs and the 4 plates from the transmission case.
noteCheck the number and order of flange, discs and plates.
 

 
 
2. BRAKE CYLINDER NO. 1 WITH BRAKE PISTON NO. 1 REMOVAL
1.
caution
  • Be careful not to shorten the return spring too much.
  • Be careful not to damage the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 1.
  • Be careful not to damage the transmission case.
Insert screwdrivers to the space between the snap ring and transmission case, and release the fitting gradually to remove the snap ring from the transmission case.
2. Remove the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 1 and the brake cylinder No. 1 with brake piston No. 1 from the transmission case.
 

 
 
3. ONE-WAY CLUTCH ASSEMBLY NO. 3 WITH INNER RACE REMOVAL
1. Using snap ring pliers, remove the snap ring from the transmission case.
2. Remove the intermediate shaft with one-way clutch assembly No. 3 with inner race and RR planetary ring gear flange with RR planetary ring gear from the transmission case.
3. Remove the one-way clutch assembly No. 3 with inner race from the RR planetary ring gear flange with RR planetary ring gear.
 

 
 
4. RR PLANETARY RING GEAR FLANGE WITH RR PLANETARY RING GEAR REMOVAL
1. Remove the RR planetary ring gear flange with RR planetary ring gear from the intermediate shaft.
2. Remove the thrust needle roller bearing and the 2 thrust bearing races from the intermediate shaft.
 

 
 
5. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 4 (B4) REMOVAL
Remove the 2 flanges, the 7 discs and the 6 plates from the transmission case.
noteCheck the number and order of flanges, discs and plates.

INSPECTION BEFORE REASSEMBLY
Refer to Brake Disc and Intermediate Shaft Inspection .
  • Inspect Brake Disc Set No. 4 (B4)
  • Check Pack Clearance of Brake Disc Set No. 4 (B4)
  • Inspect Intermediate Shaft
  • Check One-way Clutch Assembly No. 3
  • Check Brake Piston Return Spring Sub-assembly No. 1
  • Inspect Brake Disc Set No. 1 (B1)
  • Check Pack Clearance of Brake Disc Set No. 1 (B1)
 
 
1. RR PLANETARY RING GEAR FLANGE WITH RR PLANETARY RING GEAR AND INTERMEDIATE SHAFT INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the thrust needle roller bearing and the 2 thrust bearing races, and install them to the intermediate shaft.
2. Install the RR planetary ring gear flange with RR planetary ring gear to the intermediate shaft.
3. Install the intermediate shaft with the RR planetary ring gear flange with RR planetary ring gear to the transmission case.
 

 
 
2. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 4 (B4) INSTALLATION
cautionCheck the number and order of flanges, discs and plates (Refer to Brake Disc and Intermediate Shaft Inspection - Check Pack Clearance of Brake Disc Set No. 4 (B4) ).
Install the 2 flanges, the 7 discs and the 6 plates to the transmission case.
 

 
 
3. ONE-WAY CLUTCH ASSEMBLY NO. 3 WITH INNER RACE INSTALLATION
1. Install the one-way clutch assembly No. 3 with inner race to the transmission case.
2.
caution
  • Install the snap ring so that the tapered side is facing out.
  • Be sure to install the snap ring so that the ends of the snap ring are within the area shown in the illustration.
Using the snap ring pliers, install the snap ring to the transmission case.
 

 
 
4. BRAKE CYLINDER NO. 1 WITH BRAKE PISTON NO. 1 INSTALLATION
Install the brake cylinder No. 1 with brake piston No. 1 to the transmission case while making sure that the 2 claws of the brake cylinder No. 1 with brake piston No. 1 are positioned as shown in the illustration.
 

 
 
5. BRAKE PISTON RETURN SPRING SUB-ASSEMBLY NO. 1 INSTALLATION
1. Align the 2 claws of the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 1 with the 2 claws of the brake cylinder No. 1, and then install the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 1 to the brake piston No. 1.
2.
caution
  • Be careful not to shorten the return spring too much.
  • Do not directly press down the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 1 with a screwdriver. Otherwise, the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 1 may be deformed. Make sure to press down the snap ring with the screwdriver.
  • Never reuse the deformed brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 1.
  • Be careful not to damage the transmission case.
  • Be sure to install the snap ring so that the ends of the snap ring are within the area shown in the illustration.
Install the snap ring to the transmission case while pressing the snap ring and brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 1 with screwdrivers.
 

 
 
6. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 1 (B1) INSTALLATION
1.
cautionCheck the number and order of flange, discs and plates (Refer to Brake Disc and Intermediate Shaft Inspection - Check Pack Clearance of Brake Disc Set No. 1 (B1) ).
Install the flange, the 4 discs and the 4 plates to the transmission case as shown in the illustration.
2.
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the transmission case.
  • Be sure to install the snap ring so that the ends of the snap ring are within the area shown in the illustration.
Using a screwdriver, install the snap ring to the transmission case.
