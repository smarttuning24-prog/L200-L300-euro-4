DISASSEMBLY AND REASSEMBLY
 
 
1.Torque converter assembly
2.Converter housing
 
 
DISASSEMBLY
 
 
1. TORQUE CONVERTER ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the oil pump oil seal.
  • Be careful not to drop the torque converter assembly.
Remove the torque converter assembly from the transmission assembly.
 

 
 
2. CONVERTER HOUSING REMOVAL
1.
cautionBe careful not to damage the fitting surface of the converter housing and the transmission case.
Remove the 12 bolts and the converter housing from the transmission assembly.
Bolt size:
A: M10 × 1.5 × 35 mm
B: M10 × 1.5 × 35 mm (Seal bolt)
C: M12 × 1.75 × 38 mm (Seal bolt)
 
 
 
 
REASSEMBLY
 
 
1. CONVERTER HOUSING INSTALLATION
1. Clean sealant and oil off the converter housing contact surface of the transmission case and the 12 bolt holes.
2.
cautionBe careful not to damage the fitting surface of the converter housing and the transmission case.
Install the converter housing to the transmission case.
noteHit all around contact surface with a plastic hammer during installation.
3. Temporarily tighten the 8 bolts (bolt: A), and 4 new bolts (bolt: B, C) by hand as shown in the illustration.
Bolt size:
  • A: M10 × 1.5 × 35 mm
  • B: M10 × 1.5 × 35 mm (Seal bolt)
  • C: M12 × 1.75 × 38 mm (Seal bolt)
noteThe letter A, B and C shown in the illustration indicate the kind of bolts.
4.
caution
  • Be sure to tighten bolts A before tightening bolts B and C.
  • Be sure to tighten bolts A evenly and gradually, in a diagonally opposing sequence.
Tighten the 12 bolts to the specified torque.
Tightening torque:
  • A, B: 34 ± 7 N·m
  • C: 57 ± 9 N·m
 

 
 
2. TORQUE CONVERTER ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the oil pump oil seal.
  • Be careful not to drop the torque converter assembly.
  • Be careful not to pinch fingers.
Using a screwdriver, position the drive gear on the oil pump assembly in the centre. Then install the torque converter assembly on the transmission assembly.
 

 
 
3. INSPECT INSTALLATION POSITION OF TORQUE CONVERTER ASSEMBLY
Measure the dimension from the end face of the converter housing to the torque converter assembly as shown in the illustration, and check that the torque converter assembly is installed properly.
Standard value: More than 33.5 mm
(Length A)
