Camshaft <4N14 and 4N15 (Except MIVEC)>
1. Measure the cam height (cam long diameter). If it goes beyond the limit value, replace the camshaft.
Standard value
  • Inlet: 36.596 mm <4N14>
  • Inlet: 36.414 mm <4N15 (Except MIVEC)>
  • Exhaust: 36.669 mm
Limit
  • Inlet: 36.096 mm <4N14>
  • Inlet: 35.914 mm <4N15 (Except MIVEC)>
  • Exhaust: 36.169 mm
