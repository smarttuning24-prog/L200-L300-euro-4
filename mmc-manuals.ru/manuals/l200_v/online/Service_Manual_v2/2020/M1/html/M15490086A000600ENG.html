Inspection procedure 7: One of the door switch signals is not received.
 
 
cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input signal circuit is normal.
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
If there is an error to the rear door switch input signal, the rear door switch signal is no longer output to the communication line.
noteWhen the right, left rear door is opened, the signal is also displayed as "Rear door switch". Therefore, when "Rear door switch" signal is displayed as abnormal, you should determine which signal line is defective by opening the rear doors one at a time.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of rear door switch (LH)
  • Malfunction of rear door switch (RH)
  • Malfunction of the ETACS-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. M.U.T.-III data list
Check signals from each door.
(1) Open the driver's and passenger's doors.

19Driver's door switchON
48Passenger's door switchON
OK: Normal condition is displayed.
(2) Open the rear door (RH) with the other doors closed.
 
Item No.Item nameNormal condition
49Rear door switchON
OK: Normal condition is displayed.
(3) Open the rear door (LH) with the other doors closed.
 
Item No.Item nameNormal condition
49Rear door switchON
OK: Normal condition is displayed.
Is the check result normal?
YES (Normal condition is displayed) : Go to Step 6.
NO (Normal condition is not displayed for item No. 19) : Refer to Inspection Procedure 5 "The front door switch (driver’s side) signal is not received ."
NO (Normal condition is not displayed for item No. 48) : Refer to Inspection Procedure 6 "The front door switch (passenger’s side) signal is not received ."
NO (When any of the rear doors is opened, normal condition is not displayed for item No. 49 for only a respective door) : Go to Step 2.
NO (When each of the rear doors is opened, normal condition is displayed for item No. 49 for none of the doors) : Go to Step 5.
 
STEP 2. Check the installation condition.
Check that the rear door switch is installed to the body correctly.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 3.
Correct the installation condition.
 
STEP 3. Check the rear door switch.
Check the rear door switches. (Refer to GROUP 42A - Door Assembly <Door Switch>.)
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 4.
Replace the rear door switch (Refer to GROUP 42A - Door Assembly <Double Cab>).
 
STEP 4. Check the wiring harness between each rear door switch connector and the ETACS-ECU connector.
  • Check of short to power supply, short to earth, and open circuit in DOOR line between ETACS-ECU connector and rear door switch (RH). <Rear door (RH)>
  • Check of short to power supply, short to earth, and open circuit in DOOR line between ETACS-ECU connector and rear door switch (LH) connector. <Rear door (LH)>
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 6.
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 5. Connector check: ETACS-ECU connector (DOOR terminal)
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 6.
Repair the defective connector.
 
STEP 6. M.U.T.-III data list
Open each of the rear doors.

49Rear door switchON
OK: When each of the doors is opened, normal condition is displayed for all.
Is the check result normal?
