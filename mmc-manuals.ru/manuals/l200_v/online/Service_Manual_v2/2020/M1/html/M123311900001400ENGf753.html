1. INSPECT CLUTCH DISC SET NO. 1 (C1)
Check the lining of all discs.
note
  • If the lining is flaking or has changed color, or if it is worn or the print mark is wearing away, replace with a new disc. When replacing, check the contact surfaces between the flange and plate and the disc. If they are scratched or have changed color, replace with new parts.
  • Before replacing with new discs, soak them at least 2 hours in ATF.
 

 
 
2. CHECK FORWARD CLUTCH RETURN SPRING SUB-ASSEMBLY
cautionIf the free length is smaller than the minimum, replace the forward clutch return spring sub-assembly with a new one.
Using vernier calipers, measure the free length of the forward clutch return spring sub-assembly.
Standard value: 26.74 mm
(For forward clutch return spring sub-assembly)
 

 
 
3. INSPECT CLUTCH DISC SET NO. 2 (C2)
Check the lining of all discs.
note
  • If the lining is flaking or has changed color, or if it is worn or the print mark is wearing away, replace with a new disc. When replacing, check the contact surfaces between the flange and plate and the disc. If they are scratched or have changed color, replace with new parts.
  • Before replacing with new discs, soak them at least 2 hours in ATF.
 

 
 
4. CHECK DIRECT CLUTCH RETURN SPRING SUB-ASSEMBLY
cautionIf the free length is smaller than the minimum, replace the direct clutch return spring sub-assembly with a new one.
Using vernier calipers, measure the free length of the direct clutch return spring sub-assembly.
Standard value: 22.14 mm
(For direct clutch return spring sub-assembly)
 

 
 
5. CHECK FORWARD CLUTCH DRUM SUB-ASSEMBLY BUSHING
caution
  • Measure the inside diameter at several places and take an average. (More than 3 places) If the average is greater than the maximum, replace the forward clutch drum sub-assembly with a new one.
  • When the forward clutch drum sub-assembly is replaced, inspect the surface in contact the intermediate shaft. If the surface is scratched or discolored, replace the intermediate shaft with a new one.
Using a dial indicator, measure the inside diameter of the forward clutch drum sub-assembly bushing.
Standard value: 20.000 - 20.025 mm
 

 
 
6. CHECK C2 CLUTCH DRUM SUB-ASSEMBLY BUSHING
caution
  • Measure the inside diameter at several places and take an average. (More than 3 places) If the average is greater than the maximum, replace the C2 clutch drum sub-assembly with a new one.
  • When the C2 clutch drum sub-assembly is replaced, inspect the surface in contact the oil pump assembly (stator shaft). If the surface is scratched or discolored, replace the oil pump assembly (stator shaft) with a new one.
Using a dial indicator, measure the inside diameter of the C2 clutch drum sub-assembly bushing.
Standard value: 59.515 - 59.540 mm
