REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS
 
 
<<A>> SUNVISOR HOLDER REMOVAL
Insert the special tools ornament remover (MB990784) into the sunvisor holder from its left and right sides to disengage the claws on the sunvisor holder sides.
 

 
 
<<B>> HEADLINING REMOVAL
1. To remove the clip, insert a vinyl cord into a clearance between the clip and the headlining, and pass it around the clip. Then, pull out the clip downwards while pulling the vinyl cord.
2. To remove the headlining from the vehicle, remove from the rear door (steering wheel side) as shown in the figure. <Double cab>
