REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
 
 
noteThe ASTC-ECU is integrated in the hydraulic unit.
cautionWhen the hydraulic unit (integrated with ASTC-ECU) is replaced, always carry out the calibration of the master cylinder pressure sensor, steering wheel sensor and the G and yaw rate sensor (Refer to ).
Pre-removal Operation
  • Brake Fluid Draining
  • Battery Removal (Refer to GROUP 54A - Battery ) <L.H. drive vehicles>
  • Air Cleaner Removal (Refer to GROUP 15 - Air Cleaner )
Post-installation Operation
  • Brake Fluid Filling
  • Air Cleaner Installation (Refer to GROUP 15 - Air Cleaner )
  • Battery Installation (Refer to GROUP 54A - Battery ) <L.H. drive vehicles>
  • Brake Line Bleeding (Refer to GROUP 35A - On-vehicle Service, Bleeding )
  • Hydraulic Unit Check (Refer to )
  • All Sensor Calibration (Refer to )
Removal steps
1.Harness connector
>>A<<2.Brake tube and hydraulic unit assembly connection
3.Brake tube (MP)
4.Brake tube (MS)
5.Hydraulic unit assembly and hydraulic unit bracket assembly
6.Insulator
7.Collar
<<A>>8.Hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU)
9.Hydraulic unit bracket assembly
 
 
REMOVAL SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> HYDRAULIC UNIT ASSEMBLY (INTEGRATED WITH ASTC-ECU) REMOVAL
caution
  • The hydraulic unit assembly is heavy. Use care when removing it.
  • The hydraulic unit cannot be disassembled. Never loosen its nuts or bolts.
  • Do not drop or shock the hydraulic unit.
  • Do not turn the hydraulic unit upside down or lay it on its side.
