REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS
 
 
<<A>> OIL PAN SUB-ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
1.
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the fitting surfaces of the transmission case and the oil pan sub-assembly.
  • Be careful not to deform the oil pan sub-assembly.
Using a plastic hammer, tap the oil pan sub-assembly to remove it.

 
 
<<B>> VALVE BODY ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
cautionBe careful not to drop the valve body assembly.
 
 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS
 
 
>>A<< VALVE BODY ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Insert the manual valve lever into the groove on the end of the manual valve.
2. Install the valve body assembly with 12 new bolts to the transmission case.
Bolt size:
A: M6 × 1.0 × 25 mm
B: M6 × 1.0 × 36 mm
C: M6 × 1.0 × 50 mm
3. Tighten the 12 bolts to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 11 ± 1 N·m
 

 
 
>>B<< OIL PAN GASKET/OIL PAN SUB-ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Clean oil off the oil pan gasket fitting surface of the transmission case and the 10 bolt holes.
noteCompletely remove oil with white gasoline or similar.
2.
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the fitting surfaces of the transmission case and the oil pan sub-assembly.
  • Be careful not to deform the oil pan sub-assembly.
Install a new oil pan gasket and the transmission oil pan sub-assembly to the transmission case.
3.
caution
  • Make sure that there is no oil or foreign matter on the oil pan gasket seal surface and the oil pan sub-assembly contact surface.
  • Install the oil pan gasket so that there is no slack in the oil pan gasket, and that the seal surface's entire circumference is level.
  • Make sure that the oil pan gasket drop prevention protrusions are set on the oil pan sub-assembly.
  • When tightening the oil pan sub-assembly, make sure that the oil pan gasket is not pinched between the oil pan gasket tightening area's sleeve and the transmission case seal surface.
Install the 10 bolts to the transmission case.
Bolt size:
M6 × 1.0 × 25 mm
4. Tighten the 10 bolts to the specified torque.
noteBe sure to tighten the bolts evenly and gradually, in a diagonally opposing sequence.
Tightening torque: 7.4 ± 1.4 N·m
