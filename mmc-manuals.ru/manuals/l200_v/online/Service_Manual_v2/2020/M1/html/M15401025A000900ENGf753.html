Inspection Procedure 2: None of the high-beam headlamps illuminate.
cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input and output signal circuits are normal.
 
 
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
If none of the high-beam headlamps illuminate, the headlamp switch input circuit, the headlamp relay (HI) or the ETACS-ECU may be defective.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of the headlamp relay (HI)
  • Malfunction of the column switch
  • Malfunction of the ETACS-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
