Code No. P1612: Starter Relay for Auto Start <A/T>
 
 
 
 
OPERATION
  • Power for starter drive is supplied from the battery to the starter relay connector terminal.
  • At the auto start, battery voltage is supplied to the starter relay coil connector terminal from the AS&G-ECU connector terminal STR.
  • When the starter relay is turned ON, battery voltage is supplied to the starter from the starter relay connector terminal.
 
 
FUNCTION
The AS&G-ECU turns ON the starter relay at the auto start. This, in turn, supplies battery voltage to the starter.
 
 
TROUBLE JUDGMENT
Check Condition
  • More than 2 seconds have passed since the ignition switch is turn to the "ON" position or more than 2 seconds have passed since engine starting sequence was completed.
  • Battery voltage is 10 V or more and 16 V or less.
  • Not in auto start.
Judgement Criterion
  • The starter relay (for auto start) terminal monitor voltage is 4.5 V or more for 2 seconds.
 
 
FAIL-SAFE AND BACKUP FUNCTION
In the normal operation, the auto stop is prohibited. If the malfunction is detected during the auto stop, the auto start is prohibited.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Open/short circuit or damage in starter relay (auto start) circuit or loose connector contact.
  • Failed AS&G-ECU.
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. Check of short circuit to power supply, short to earth, open circuit and harness damage in STR line between AS&G-ECU connector and starter relay connector
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 2.
Repair the damaged harness wire.
 
STEP 2. Diagnosis code recheck after erasing diagnosis code
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Check again if the diagnosis code is set.
(1) Use M.U.T.-III to erase the diagnosis code.
(2) Turn the ignition switch to "LOCK" (OFF) position.
(3) Turn the ignition switch to "ON" position and start the engine.
(4) Use M.U.T.-III to recheck the diagnosis code.
Is diagnosis code No. P1612 set?
Replace the AS&G-ECU.
Intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunctions ).
