Code No. B1301: DC/DC Converter (Entertainment System) Performance <A/T>
 
 
 
 
OPERATION
  • At the auto start, the AS&G-ECU connector terminal DCCT sends increasing voltage request signal to the DC/DC converter (entertainment system) connector terminal DCCT.
  • If the increasing voltage control system is abnormal, the DC/DC converter (entertainment system) connector terminal DCCA should send the diagnostic signal to the AS&G-ECU connector terminal DCDA.
 
 
FUNCTION
The AS&G-ECU checks whether the increasing voltage control system is normal.
 
 
TROUBLE JUDGEMENT
Check Condition
  • Ignition switch is "ON" position.
  • The IOD fuse is installed.
Judgement Criterion
  • For 5 seconds, the duty signal (failure signal) is received in a row.
 
 
FAIL-SAFE AND BACKUP FUNCTION
None.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Connection with accessories having rated output current large enough to possibly cause overloads (overcurrents).
  • Modified circuit.
  • Failed battery.
  • Open/short circuit or harness damage in DC/DC converter (entertainment system) circuit or loose connector contact.
  • Failed DC/DC converter (entertainment system).
  • Failed AS&G-ECU.
