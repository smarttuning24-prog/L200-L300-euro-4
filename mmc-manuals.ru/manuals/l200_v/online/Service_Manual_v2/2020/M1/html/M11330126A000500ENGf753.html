REMOVAL SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> COMMON RAIL ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
caution
  • When operating, do not support the common rail assembly by holding the rail pressure sensor and the fuel pressure relief control valve at both ends.
  • Before the installation, make sure that there is no foreign object or rust to the thread and to the high-pressure pipe sheet surface.
 
 
 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT
 
 
>>A<< COMMON RAIL ASSEMBLY/FUEL INJECTION PIPE NO.4/FUEL INJECTION PIPE NO.2/FUEL INJECTION PIPE NO.3/FUEL INJECTION PIPE NO.1/SUPPLY PUMP PIPE/FUEL RETURN PIPE B/FUEL RETURN GASKET/EYE BOLT INSTALLATION
1. Install the common rail assembly to the cylinder head, and let the mounting bolt be seated (until the bolt slightly touches the common rail installation surface).
2.
caution
  • The removed fuel injection pipe and supply pump pipe can be reassembled up to 5 times. To count the number of fuel injection pipe and supply pump pipe reassembly, record it by adding the reassembly number of this time (once usually) to the previous number of reassembly written on the maintenance handbook. When the number of the previous reassembly reaches 5 or when the fuel injector assembly or common rail assembly is replaced, always use a new fuel injection pipe and supply pump pipe. At that time, write "New (0 times)" in the maintenance handbook.
  • If a fuel leak is present in the fuel injection pipe and supply pump pipe once, do not reuse it and replace with a new one.
  • If the common rail assembly is replaced, the contact surface (seal surface) between the fuel injection pipe or supply pump pipe and the common rail assembly is changed. So do not reuse the fuel injection pipe and fuel supply pump pipe and replace with a new one.
  • When reassembling the fuel injection pipe and supply pump pipe, remove the foreign material attached on the sealing surface, and install it with the contacting point of the pipe aligned.
Align the fuel injection pipe and supply pump pipe centre axis with the other side components (common rail assembly, fuel injector assembly and supply pump assembly) centre axis to install the fuel injection pipe No.1 to No.4 and supply pump pipe. Then, tighten the flare nut temporarily until the tip of pipe (seal surface) contacts with the other side components.
noteWhen the fuel injection pipe and supply pump pipe are installed, tighten the flare nut while shaking the pipe lightly to align the centre axis of both components.
3. Temporarily install the fuel return pipe B to the rocker cover.
4. Temporarily install the supply pump pipe to the intake manifold.
5. Tighten the supply pump pipe mounting bolts, which are located on the intake manifold side of the temporarily installed supply pump pipe, to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 9.5 ± 2.5 N·m
6. Using special tool crowfoot wrench (MB992211), tighten the common rail assembly side of the fuel injection pipe No.1 to No.4 and supply pump pipe to the temporary tightening torque calculated by the following formula.
Tightening torque: (18 ± 2) × {L ÷ (L + 25)} N·m
  • 18 ± 2: Tightening torque of the fuel injection pipe (unit: N·m)
  • L: Distance between the centre of a torque wrench drive and of its handle (unit: mm)
  • 25: Length of pitch for special tool between holes (unit: mm)
7. Using special tool MB992211, tighten the common rail assembly side of the fuel injection pipe No. 1 to No. 4 and supply pump pipe to the specified torque calculated by the following formula.
Tightening torque: (35 ± 5) × {L ÷ (L + 25)} N·m
  • 35 ± 5: Tightening torque of the fuel injection pipe (unit: N·m)
  • L: Distance between the centre of a torque wrench drive and of its handle (unit: mm)
  • 25: Length of pitch for special tool between holes (unit: mm)
8. Replace the fuel return gasket with a new one.
9. Install the fuel return pipe B to the common rail assembly and tighten the eye bolt to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 20 ± 2 N·m
10. Tighten the fuel return pipe B mounting bolt, which is located on the rocker cover side of the temporarily installed fuel return pipe B, to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 9.5 ± 2.5 N·m
11. Tighten the common rail assembly to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 25 ± 7 N·m
12. Check that no fuel is leaked from the pipe connection area and the fuel return gasket.
