Inspection Procedure 4: Although AS&G OFF Switch Pressed, AS&G System Not Switched
 
 
 
 
OPERATION
  • The battery voltage is supplied to the AS&G OFF switch connector terminal from the ignition switch connector terminal IG1 <vehicles without KOS> or the IG1 relay connector terminal <vehicles with KOS>.
  • The ON/OFF signal is inputted to the AS&G-ECU connector terminal ASGM from the AS&G OFF switch connector terminal.
 
 
FUNCTION
Pressing the AS&G OFF switch can optionally turn OFF the AS&G system or turn ON the AS&G system.
 
 
COMMENT ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
The failure could possibly be caused by the failed AS&G OFF switch circuit and so on.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Failed AS&G OFF switch.
  • Open/short circuit or harness damage in the AS&G OFF switch circuit or loose connector contact.
  • Failed AS&G-ECU.
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. M.U.T.-III data list
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Use M.U.T.-III to check the data list (Refer to ).
  • Item No. 5: AS&G OFF SW
OK:
  • ON [AS&G OFF switch: Pressed (only while the switch is pressed)]
  • OFF (AS&G OFF switch: Released)
Is the check result normal?
Intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunctions ).
Go to Step 2.
 
STEP 2. Check the AS&G OFF switch itself
Check the AS&G OFF switch itself (Refer to ).
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 3.
Replace the AS&G OFF switch.
 
STEP 3. Perform voltage measurement at AS&G OFF switch connector
  • Disconnect connector, and measure at harness side.
  • Ignition switch: "ON" position
  • Voltage between terminal AS&G OFF switch IG1 line and body earth.
OK: System voltage
Is the check result normal?
YES <Vehicles without KOS> : Go to Step 4.
YES <Vehicles with KOS> : Go to Step 5.
NO <Vehicles without KOS> : Repair the connector(s), or check and repair of short to earth and open circuit in IG1 line between ignition switch connector and AS&G OFF switch connector.
NO <Vehicles with KOS> : Repair the connector(s), or check and repair of short to earth and open circuit in IG1 line between IG1 relay connector and AS&G OFF switch connector.
 
STEP 4. Check of harness damage in IG1 line between ignition switch connector and AS&G OFF switch connector <Vehicles without KOS>
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 6.
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 5. Check of harness damage in IG1 line between IG1 relay connector and AS&G OFF switch connector <Vehicles with OKS>
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 6.
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 6. Check of short to power supply, short to earth, open circuit and harness damage in ASGM line between AS&G OFF switch connector and AS&G-ECU connector
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 7.
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 7. Check the trouble symptom
Does the trouble symptom persist?
Replace the AS&G-ECU.
Intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunctions ).
