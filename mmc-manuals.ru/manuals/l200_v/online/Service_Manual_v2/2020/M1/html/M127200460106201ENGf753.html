GEAR OIL CHANGE
2020 / L200/L200 SPORTERO / EU - Workshop Manual
27 -REAR AXLE > ON-VEHICLE SERVICE > GEAR OIL CHANGE
 
 
 
 
Vehicle is lifted (not standing on wheels)
1. Remove the filler plug and gasket.
2. Remove the drain plug and gasket, and drain the oil.
3. Tighten the drain plug and new gasket, to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 60 ± 10 N·m
4. Fill the Mitsubishi Motors Genuine Super Hypoid Gear Oil ECOLOGY API GL-5 SAE 80 until the level satisfies the specification.
 
ItemDimension A
Size and number of the nuts securing the differential carrier assemblyM10 × 12Within 38 mm
Specified gear oil:
  • Mitsubishi Motors Genuine Super Hypoid Gear Oil ECOLOGY API GL-5 SAE 80
  • Amount to use:
    Size and number of the nuts securing the differential carrier assembly (M10×12): 2.3 L
5. Install the filler plug and new gasket, then tighten them to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 50 ± 10 N·m
