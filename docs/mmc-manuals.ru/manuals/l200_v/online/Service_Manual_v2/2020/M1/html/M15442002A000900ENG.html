REMOVAL SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> ANTENNA ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
1. Pull out the feeder cable slightly until the tube end of the antenna can been seen.
cautionMake sure that the cord is not loosened.
2. Insert a cord into the tube end, and secure the cord with plastic tape as shown.
3. Remove the antenna assembly by pulling it gradually.
