ENGINE OIL TEMPERATURE SENSOR CHECK <4N14>
2020 / L200/L200 SPORTERO / EU - Workshop Manual
13 -FUEL > 13A-DIESEL FUEL > ON-VEHICLE SERVICE > ENGINE OIL TEMPERATURE SENSOR CHECK<4N14>
1.
cautionDo not tilt the special tool oil pressure sensor wrench (MB992118) as the engine oil pressure and temperature sensor connector may be damaged.
Use special tool oil pressure switch wrench (MB992118) to remove the engine oil pressure and temperature sensor.
2. With temperature sensing portion of engine oil pressure and temperature sensor immersed in hot engine oil, check the resistance between terminal No. 3 and No.4.
Standard value:
  • 91.3 to 106.0 kΩ (at -20°C)
  • 30.7 to 34.9 kΩ (at 0°C)
  • 11.7 to 13.2 kΩ (at 20°C)
  • 5.0 to 5.6 kΩ (at 40°C)
  • 2.3 to 2.6 kΩ (at 60°C)
  • 1.2 to 1.3 kΩ (at 80°C)
  • 0.64 to 0.72 kΩ (at 100°C)
  • 0.37 to 0.41 kΩ (at 120°C)
  • 0.22 to 0.25 kΩ (at 140°C)
3. If the resistance deviates from the standard value greatly, replace the sensor.
4.
caution
  • Do not reuse the O-ring.
  • Install the O-ring after wrap a tape not having adhesion (such as a seal tape) round the notch on the engine oil pressure and temperature sensor to prevent it from damage. The damaged O-ring may cause oil leakage.
Install the O-ring to the engine oil pressure and temperature sensor.
5.
cautionTo install the engine oil pressure and temperature sensor, be careful to keep foreign material from entering oil channels.
Wipe off the dirt on the engine oil pressure and temperature sensor and engine oil pressure and temperature sensor mounting surface.
6. Apply a little amount of engine oil to the O-ring.
7. Install the engine oil pressure and temperature sensor to the connector.
8. In the same manner as removal, use special tool oil pressure switch wrench (MB992118) to tighten the engine oil pressure and temperature sensor to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 20 ± 4 N·m
