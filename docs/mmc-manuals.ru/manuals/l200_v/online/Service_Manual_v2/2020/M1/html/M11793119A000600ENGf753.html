Code No. P205E: Urea Tank Temperature Sensor Circuit Intermittent/Erratic
 
 
 
 
OPERATION
  • A power is applied to the urea tank level sensor/urea tank temperature sensor power supply terminal of urea pump module connector from the DCU connector terminal TLSS and earthed to the DCU connector terminal TLSE from the urea tank level sensor/urea tank temperature sensor earth terminal of urea pump module connector.
  • The PWM signal of sensor is inputted to the DCU connector terminal TLS from the urea tank level sensor/urea tank temperature sensor output terminal of urea pump module connector.
 
 
FUNCTION
The DCU sets diagnosis code No. P205E when it detects a characteristic abnormality of the urea tank temperature sensor.
noteWhen urea tank temperature is less than -11°C, AdBlue™ will be frozen. When AdBlue™ in tank isn't frozen, urea tank level can be detected.
 
 
TROUBLE JUDGEMENT
Check Conditions
  • Ignition switch is "ON" position.
  • DCU supply voltage is 10 V or more.
  • At least 1 second has passed since the DCU was activated.
  • The remaining amount in the urea tank is 15% or more (tank level can be detected).
Judgement Criterion
  • The urea tank temperature remains -14.4°C or less for at least 8 seconds.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
Failed urea tank assembly (integrated with urea tank temperature sensor)
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
Replace the urea tank assembly (integrated with urea tank temperature sensor) (Refer to )
