Inspection Procedure 11: The rear window defogger switch signal is not received. <vehicles with cooler or manual air conditioner>
 
 
cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the input signal circuit is normal.
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
Input signal from the rear window defogger switch is used to operate the functions below. If the signal is abnormal, these functions will not work normally.
  • Rear window defogger
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of the heater control panel (rear window defogger switch)
  • Malfunction of the ETACS-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. Check the rear window defogger switch.
Refer to .
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 2.
Replace the heater control panel (Refer to GROUP 55A - Heater Control Unit ).
 
STEP 2. Resistance measurement at heater control panel connector (earth terminal)
(1) Disconnect the connector, and measure at the wiring harness side.
(2) Measure the resistance between the heater control panel connector (earth terminal) and body earth.
OK: Continuity exists (2 Ω or less)
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 4.
Go to Step 3.
 
STEP 3. Check of open circuit in earth line between heater control panel connector and body earth
Is the check result normal?
Intermittent malfunction is suspected (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 4. Check of short to power supply, short to earth, and open circuit in DFSW line between ETACS-ECU connector and heater control panel connector
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 5.
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 5. M.U.T.-III data list
Check the input signals from the rear window defogger switch
  • Operate the rear window defogger switch.

OK: Normal condition is displayed.
Is the check result normal?
The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to use Troubleshooting/inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Replace the ETACS-ECU (Refer to ).
