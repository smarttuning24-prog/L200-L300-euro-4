Code No. P0268: No. 3 Injector Circuit High <4N14>
 
 
 
 
OPERATION
Refer to Code P0203: No. 3 Injector Circuit Malfunction .
 
 
FUNCTION
The engine-ECU monitors peak current during injector drive control to detect disconnection in the No. 3 fuel injector circuit.
 
 
TROUBLE JUDGEMENT
Check Conditions
  • 2 seconds later after the ignition switch has been in "ON" position.
  • Battery positive voltage is 8 to 16 V.
  • During fuel injection
  • Diagnosis code No. P126A or P126B is not set.
Judgement Criterion
  • Overcurrent (coil short) in the No. 3 fuel injector circuit is detected.
 
 
FAIL-SAFE AND BACKUP FUNCTION
  • The throttle valve is opened (Electrical discontinuity).
  • The EGR control is stopped (fully closed).
  • The DPF regeneration is prohibited.
  • The AS&G control is prohibited.
  • The small injection quantity learning is prohibited.
  • The speed limiter control is prohibited.
  • The cruise control is prohibit.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Harness damage in No. 3 fuel injector circuit or loose connector contact.
  • Failed No. 3 fuel injector assembly.
  • Failed engine-ECU.
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
warningA high-tension voltage is flowing between the engine-ECU and the fuel injectors while the engine is running. Handle them carefully to avoid receiving electric shocks from the voltage.
STEP 1. Check No. 3 fuel injector assembly itself
  • No. 3 fuel injector connector check.
  • Check No. 3 fuel injector assembly itself (Refer to ).
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 2.
Repair or replace the connector, or replace the No. 3 fuel injector assembly.
 
STEP 2. Check harness between No. 3 fuel injector connector terminal and engine-ECU connector terminal IB3, and No. 3 fuel injector connector terminal and engine-ECU connector terminal INJ3
Check power supply line and earthing line for short circuit.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace the connector, or repair the damaged harness wire.
 
STEP 3. Diagnosis code recheck after erasing diagnosis code
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Check again if the diagnosis code is set.
(1) Use M.U.T.-III to erase the diagnosis code.
(2) Turn the ignition switch to "LOCK" (OFF) position.
(3) Turn the ignition switch to "ON" position and start the engine.
(4) Use M.U.T.-III to recheck the diagnosis code.
Is diagnosis code No. P0268 set?
Replace the engine-ECU.
Intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Use Troubleshooting/Inspection Service Points, How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunctions ).
