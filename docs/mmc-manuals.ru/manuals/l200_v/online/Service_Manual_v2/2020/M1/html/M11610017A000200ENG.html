Rotor
Check the rotor coil continuity. Make sure that there is continuity between the slip rings. Measure the rotor resistance. If it is excessively small, it indicates a shorted rotor. If there is no continuity or if it is shorted, replace the rotor assembly.
Standard value: 1.4 to 1.7 Ω
 
 
 
Stator
1. Check the electrical continuity between the stator coil terminals.
2. Check the electrical continuity of the points shown in the table below. Confirm that there is electrical continuity.
If there is not, replace the Alternator assembly.

Brush
Measure the length of the protrusion of the brush. Replace the brush holder assembly if the protrusion length is shorter than the limit.
Limit: 10 mm
