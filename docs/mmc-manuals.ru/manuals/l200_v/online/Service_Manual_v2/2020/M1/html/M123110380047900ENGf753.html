HYDRAULIC PRESSURE TEST
2020 / L200/L200 SPORTERO / EU - Workshop Manual
23 -AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION > 23C-AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION<R6AWH, V6AWH> > ON-VEHICLE SERVICE > HYDRAULIC PRESSURE TESTS
 
 
This test measures the line pressure when the selector lever is in the "D" or "R" position with the engine idling or stalled. With this test, the operation status of the transmission components can be checked.
1.
warningFor safety, operators must not stand in front of and at the rear of the vehicle during this test.
Check the A/T fluid temperature.
  • A/T fluid temperature: 50 - 80°C
  • Switches for air conditioner, lamps, and others shall be OFF.
noteUse the M.U.T.-III to measure the A/T fluid temperature.
2. Raise the vehicle.
3. Remove the plug from the line pressure take off port, and install special tools as shown in the figure and place the meter inside vehicle.
  • Oil pressure gauge <3.0 MPa> (MD998330)
  • Adapter (MD998920)
4. Pull the parking brake lever, and then fully depress the brake pedal.
5. Start the engine.
6.
caution
  • Do not maintain the throttle fully opened condition for 5 seconds or longer.
  • Perform the measurement for 3 times or more, and obtain the average value of the measurements.
  • When successively performing the test under the stall state, only perform the test after checking that the A/T fluid temperature is not at 80°C or higher. If the A/T fluid temperature exceeds 80°C, perform the test after running the engine at around 1,000 r/min for 1 minute or longer and then cooling the A/T fluid to 80°C or lower.
7. Shift the selector lever to "D" and "R" position, and measure the idle state line pressure and stall state line pressure.
Standard value:
ItemLine pressure (MPa)
DR
Idling0.36 - 0.46
Stall1.58 - 1.75
 
 
 
HYDRAULIC PRESSURE TEST JUDGEMENT
Hydraulic pressure test resultProbable cause
Both "D" and "R" position are less than the standard value.
  • Malfunction of the line pressure control solenoid (SLT)
  • Malfunction of the primary regulator valve
  • Malfunction of the oil pump
  • Oile leak from "D" or "R" position oil pressure circuit
Both "D" and "R" position are higher than the standard value.
  • Malfunction of the line pressure control solenoid (SLT)
  • Malfunction of the primary regulator valve
Only "D" position is less than the standard value.
  • Malfunction of the "D" position oil pressre circuit
  • Malfunction of the clutch No. 1 (C1)
Only "D" position is higher than the standard value.
  • Malfunction of the line pressure control solenoid (SLT)
  • Malfunction of the brake No. 1 (B1)
  • Malfunction of the brake No. 4 (B4)
Only "R" position is less than the standard value.
  • Malfunction of the "R" position oil pressre circuit
Only "R" position is higher than the standard value.
  • Malfunction of the line pressure control solenoid (SLT)
  • Malfunction of the corresponding solenoid valve
