The vehicle identification code plate is riveted to the cowl top outer panel in the engine compartment.

No.Content
1Chassis No. (Vehicle identification number)
2Gross vehicle weight
3Gross combination weight
4Gross axle weight front
5Gross axle weight rear
6 or 7NTA No. (National type approval number)
