Inspection Procedure 14: ESS Inoperative or Improper Operative.
 
 
caution
  • Whenever ECU is replaced, ensure that the CAN bus lines are normal.
  • When the hydraulic unit (integrated with ASTC-ECU) is replaced, always carry out the calibration of the master cylinder pressure sensor, steering wheel sensor and the G and yaw rate sensor (Refer to ).
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
Because the ASTC system has set the diagnosis code, the ESS may be disabled.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Low battery voltage
  • The CAN bus line is defective.
  • The ASTC-ECU, which was used on another vehicle, is installed on this vehicle.
  • The ETACS-ECU, which was used on another vehicle, is installed on this vehicle.
  • Wrong coding which the ETACS-ECU has stored.
  • ETACS-ECU malfunction
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
  • Malfunction of the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU)
