DISASSEMBLY
 
 
1. OIL PUMP ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
1. Remove the 10 bolts from the oil pump assembly.
Bolt size:
M8 × 1.25 × 35 mm

2.
cautionBe careful not to drop the oil pump assembly from the transmission case.
Using special tool oil pump puller (MD998212), pull out the oil pump assembly from the transmission case.
3. Remove the 2 thrust bearing races from the oil pump assembly.
 

 
 
2. DIRECT CLUTCH ASSEMBLY & FORWARD CLUTCH ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
1.
cautionBe careful not to drop the direct clutch assembly & forward clutch assembly.
Remove the direct clutch assembly & forward clutch assembly from the transmission case.
2. Remove the 3 thrust needle roller bearings from the direct clutch assembly & the forward clutch assembly.

 
 
 
3. DIRECT CLUTCH ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
Remove the direct clutch assembly from the forward clutch assembly.
 

 
 
4. FORWARD CLUTCH HUB REMOVAL
1. Remove the forward clutch hub from the transmission case.
2. Remove the 2 thrust bearing races from the forward clutch hub.
 

 
 
5. CLUTCH HUB SUB-ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
1. Remove the clutch hub sub-assembly from the transmission case.
2. Remove the thrust needle roller bearing with race and the thrust needle roller bearing from the clutch hub sub-assembly.
 

 
 
6. SUN GEAR INPUT DRUM REMOVAL
1. Remove the sun gear input drum from the transmission case.
2. Remove the thrust needle roller bearing with race and the thrust bearing race from the sun gear input drum.
 

 
 
7. FR PLANETARY GEAR ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
1. Remove the FR planetary gear assembly from the transmission case.
2. Remove the 2 thrust bearing races from the FR planetary gear assembly.
 

 
 
8. FR PLANETARY RING GEAR WITH FR PLANETARY RING GEAR FLANGE SUB-ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
1. Remove the FR planetary ring gear with FR planetary ring gear flange sub-assembly from the transmission case.
2. Remove the thrust needle roller bearing with race and the thrust bearing race from the FR planetary ring gear with FR planetary ring gear flange sub-assembly.
 

 
 
9. BRAKE PISTON RETURN SPRING SUB-ASSEMBLY NO. 2 REMOVAL
1.
cautionBe careful not to shorten the return spring too much.
Set the following special tools and a washer (with an inner diameter of 12 mm or less) on the brake cylinder No. 2 with brake piston No. 2, and compress the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 2.
  • Puller body (MB991354)
  • Bar (MB990244)
  • Base (MB992317)
  • Centre bolt (MB992319)
  • Flange bolt (MB992322)
2.
cautionBe careful not to damage the transmission case.
Using a screwdriver, remove the snap ring from the transmission case.
3. Remove the brake cylinder No. 2 with brake piston No. 2 and brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 2 from the transmission case.
 

 
 
10. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 2 (B2) REMOVAL
Remove the 4 plates, the 5 discs and the 2 flanges from the transmission case.
noteCheck the number and order of flanges, discs and plates.
 

 
 
11. CENTRE PLANETARY RING GEAR FLANGE WITH CENTRE PLANETARY RING GEAR REMOVAL
1. Remove the centre planetary ring gear flange with centre planetary ring gear from the transmission case.
2. Remove the thrust needle roller bearing and the thrust needle roller bearing with race from the centre planetary ring gear flange with centre planetary ring gear.
 

 
 
12. CENTRE PLANETARY GEAR ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
1. Remove the centre planetary gear assembly from the transmission case.
2. Remove the thrust bearing race from the centre planetary gear assembly.
 

 
 
13. PLANETARY SUN GEAR REMOVAL
Remove the planetary sun gear from the transmission case.

INSPECTION BEFORE REASSEMBLY
Refer to Oil Pump and Centre Planetary Gear Assembly Inspection .
  • Inspect Centre Planetary Gear Assembly
  • Inspect Brake Disc Set No. 2 (B2)
  • Check Pack Clearance of Brake Disc Set No. 2 (B2)
  • Check Brake Piston Return Spring Sub-assembly No. 2
  • Inspect FR Planetary Gear Assembly
  • Inspect Sun Gear Input Drum
  • Check Clutch Hub Sub-assembly Bushing

 
 
1. PLANETARY SUN GEAR INSTALLATION
Install the planetary sun gear to the transmission case.
 

 
 
2. CENTRE PLANETARY GEAR ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the thrust bearing race and install it to the centre planetary gear assembly.
2. Install the centre planetary gear assembly to the transmission case.
 

 
 
3. CENTRE PLANETARY RING GEAR FLANGE WITH CENTRE PLANETARY RING GEAR INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the thrust needle roller bearing and the thrust needle roller bearing with race and install them to the centre planetary ring gear flange with centre planetary ring gear.
2. Install the centre planetary ring gear flange with centre planetary ring gear to the transmission case.
 

 
 
4. BRAKE DISC SET NO. 2 (B2) INSTALLATION
cautionCheck the number and order of flanges, discs and plates (Refer to Oil Pump and Centre Planetary Gear Assembly Inspection - Check Pack Clearance of Brake Disc Set No. 2 (B2) ).
Install the 4 plates, the 5 discs and the 2 flanges to the transmission case as shown in the illustration.
 

 
 
5. BRAKE PISTON RETURN SPRING SUB-ASSEMBLY NO. 2 INSTALLATION
Install the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 2 to the transmission case while making sure that the claw of the brake piston return spring sub-assembly is positioned as shown in the illustration.
 

 
 
6. BRAKE CYLINDER NO. 2 WITH BRAKE PISTON NO. 2 INSTALLATION
1. Install the brake cylinder No. 2 with brake piston No. 2 to the transmission case while making sure that the claw of the brake cylinder No. 2 with brake piston No. 2 is positioned as shown in the illustration.
2.
caution
  • Be careful not to shorten the return spring too much.
  • Be careful not to damage the brake cylinder No. 2.
Set the special tools and a washer (with an inner diameter of 12 mm or less) on the brake cylinder No. 2 with brake piston No. 2, and compress the brake piston return spring sub-assembly.
  • Puller body (MB991354)
  • Bar (MB990244)
  • Base (MB992317)
  • Centre bolt (MB992319)
  • Flange bolt (MB992322)
3.
caution
  • Be careful not to damage the transmission case.
  • Be sure to install the snap ring so that the ends of the snap ring are within the area shown in the illustration.
Using a screwdriver, install the snap ring to the transmission case.
 

 
 
7. FR PLANETARY RING GEAR WITH FR PLANETARY RING GEAR FLANGE SUB-ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the thrust needle roller bearing with race and the thrust bearing race, and install them to the FR planetary ring gear with FR planetary ring gear flange sub-assembly.
2. Install the FR planetary ring gear with FR planetary ring gear flange sub-assembly to the transmission case.
 

 
 
8. FR PLANETARY GEAR ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the 2 thrust bearing races and install them to the FR planetary gear assembly.
2. Install the FR planetary gear assembly to the transmission case.
 

 
 
9. SUN GEAR INPUT DRUM INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the thrust needle roller bearing with race and thrust bearing race, and install them to the sun gear input drum.
2. Install the sun gear input drum to the transmission case.
 

 
 
10. CLUTCH HUB SUB-ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the thrust needle roller bearing with race and the thrust needle roller bearing, and install them to the clutch hub sub-assembly.
2. Install the clutch hub sub-assembly to the transmission case.
 

 
 
11. FORWARD CLUTCH HUB INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the 2 thrust bearing races and install them to the forward clutch hub.
2. Install the forward clutch hub to the transmission case.
 

 
 
12. FORWARD CLUTCH ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the 2 thrust needle roller bearings and install them to the forward clutch assembly.
2. Install the forward clutch assembly to the transmission case.
 

 
 
13. DIRECT CLUTCH ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the thrust needle roller bearing and install it to the direct clutch assembly.
2. Install the direct clutch assembly to the transmission case.
 

 
 
14. OIL PUMP ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to the 2 thrust bearing races, and install them to the oil pump assembly.
2.
caution
  • Be careful not to drop the oil pump assembly.
  • Be careful not to damage the seal rings and transaxle oil seal ring.
Set the oil pump assembly on the direct clutch assembly & forward clutch assembly, and align the bolt holes of the oil pump assembly with the transmission case.
3. Temporarily install the oil pump assembly to the transmission case.
4. Install the 10 bolts to the oil pump assembly by tightening them to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 22 ± 3 N·m
Bolt size:
M8 × 1.25 × 35 mm
