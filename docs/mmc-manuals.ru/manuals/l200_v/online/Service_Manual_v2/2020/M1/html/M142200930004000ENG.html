REMOVAL SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> REAR DOOR GLASS ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
1. In order to protect the body (paint surface) surface, apply protective tapes to body area around the rear door glass.
2. Apply protective tapes to avoid the rear door glass assembly from dropping.
3.
Use a piano wire to cut away the adhesive of the rear door glass.
4. If the rear door glass assembly is reused, make the mating marks on the glass assembly and body, and use the special tool window glass holder (MB990480) to remove the glass assembly.
5.
caution
  • Be careful not to cut away more adhesive than is necessary.
  • Be careful not to damage a paintwork on the body surface with a knife. If the paintwork is damaged, repair the damaged area with touch-up paint or anti-rust agent.
  • If the rear door glass assembly is reused, scrape away all traces of old adhesive from the rear door glass assembly.
Use a knife to cut away the remaining adhesive so that the adhesive thickness is within 2 mm around the entire circumference of the body flange.
6. Finish the flange surfaces so that they are smooth.
 
 
 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS
 
 
>>A<< FASTENER/REAR DOOR GLASS SPACER INSTALLATION
1.
cautionLeave the degreased parts for 3 or more minutes to dry well, before starting on the next step. Do not touch the degreased parts.
Use the parts cleaner (MITSUBISHI MOTORS GENUINE Part No. MZ100387) or equivalent to degrease the inside and outside of the rear door glass and the body flange.
2.
Install the fasteners to the specified positions on the rear door glass.
3. Install the rear door glass spacer to the specified position on the rear door glass.
4. Install the fasteners to the specified positions on the body flange.
 
 
 
>>B<< REAR DOOR GARNISH ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Apply a double-sided tape to the specified position of the rear door garnish assembly.
Double-sided tape:
5 mm width, 410 mm length and 0.6 mm thickness
2. Install the rear door garnish assembly with the double-sided tape on it to the rear door glass by aligning the mating marks made at the removal step.
 

 
 
>>C<< REAR DOOR GLASS ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Apply the primer and adhesive to the rear door glass.
2. Install the glass by the same procedures as for the windshield (Refer to ).
