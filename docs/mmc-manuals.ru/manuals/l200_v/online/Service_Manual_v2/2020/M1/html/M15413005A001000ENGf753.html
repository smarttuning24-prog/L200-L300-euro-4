Inspection Procedure 2: The rear room lamp does not illuminate normally.
 
 
cautionWhenever the ECU is replaced, ensure that the power supply circuit, the earth circuit and the communication circuit are normal.
 
 
OPERATION
The ETACS-ECU illuminates and extinguishes the rear room lamp in accordance with the input signals below.
  • Ignition switch (IG1)
  • Key reminder switch
  • Door switches
  • Front door lock actuator (driver’s side)
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
If this does not work normally, the above switch input circuit(s), room lamp assembly, or ETACS-ECU may have a problem.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of the key reminder switch
  • Malfunction of door switch
  • Malfunction of the front door lock actuator (driver’s side)
  • Malfunction of room lamp assembly
  • Malfunction of the ETACS-ECU
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. Retest the system.
At which switch positions (DOOR or ON), the rear room lamp does not illuminate?
The lamp does not illuminate at both the DOOR and ON positions. : Go to Step 2.
The lamp does not illuminate at the ON position. : Go to Step 4.
The lamp does not illuminate at the DOOR position. : Go to Step 6.
 
STEP 2. Rear room lamp bulb check
Check that the rear room lamp bulb is normal.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 3.
Replace the rear room lamp bulb (Refer to ).
 
STEP 3. Check of open circuit in power supply lines between the rear room lamp connector and the fusible link.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 5.
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 4. Check of open circuit in earth line between the rear room lamp connector and the body earth.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 5.
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 5. Retest the system
Check that the rear room lamp illuminates/extinguishes normally.
Is the check result normal?
The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to use Troubleshooting/inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Replace the room lamp assembly (Refer to ).
 
STEP 6. M.U.T.-III data list
Use the ETACS-ECU data list to check the signals related to the operation of rear room lamp.
  • Turn the ignition switch to the LOCK (OFF) position.
  • Remove the ignition key from the ignition key cylinder.
  • Open each door.

Is the check result normal?
Normal conditions are displayed for all the items. : Go to Step 7.
Normal condition is not displayed for item No. 19. : Troubleshoot the ETACS-ECU. Refer to Inspection Procedure 5 "The front door switch (driver’s side) signal is not received" .

Normal condition is not displayed for item No. 28. : Troubleshoot the ETACS-ECU. Refer to Inspection Procedure 3 "Key reminder switch signal is not received" .
Normal condition is not displayed for item No. 30. : Troubleshoot the ETACS-ECU. Refer to Inspection Procedure 2 "The ignition switch (IG1) signal is not received" .
Normal condition is not displayed for item No. 48. : Troubleshoot the ETACS-ECU. Refer to Inspection Procedure 6 "The front door switch (passenger’s side) signal is not received ."
Normal condition is not displayed for item No. 49. : Troubleshoot the ETACS-ECU. Refer to Inspection Procedure 7 "One of the door switch signals is not received ."
 
STEP 7. Check of open circuit in ROOM LP lines between the rear room lamp connector and the ETACS-ECU connector.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 8.
Repair the connector(s) or wiring harness.
 
STEP 8. Retest the system
Check that the rear room lamp illuminates/extinguishes normally.
Is the check result normal?
The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to use Troubleshooting/inspection Service Points - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Replace the ETACS-ECU (Refer to ETACS-ECU ).
