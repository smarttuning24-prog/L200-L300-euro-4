TEST MODE
 
 
The emergency call switch allows the indicator, the speaker or the microphone to enter a respective test mode.
noteWhen you test a component by means of a test mode, you should park the vehicle in a well-ventilated area.
1. When the ignition switch is set to "LOCK" (OFF), set the ignition switch "ON" while pressing the emergency call switch.
2. Perform Step 3 and 4 within 20 seconds after the red lamp and green lamp illuminate.
3. Release finger from the emergency call switch.
4. Press the emergency call switch 3 or more times.
5. After approximately 20 seconds from Step 2, the red lamp and green lamp extinguish and buzzer sounds 3 times.
noteIf the buzzer does not sound, repeat the process from Step 1.
6. Approximately 60 seconds later, the green lamp flash. If press the emergency call switch within 20 seconds, the mode changes to the test mode.
7. Within approximately 20 seconds after the mode is changed to the test mode, press the emergency call switch for one of the following periods to select the desired test type.

 
 
REPORT TEST TO THE EMERGENCY CALL CENTRE or TEST OF THE ERA-GLONASS SYSTEM EQUIPMENT OF THE VEHICLE
Test typeTest contentsTest results
Indicator testThe buzzer sounds once, and then the red lamp and green lamp flash alternately every 1 second.OK (All tests): The buzzer sounds once, and the green lamp illuminates for 5 seconds.
NG (Either of the tests): The buzzer sounds three times, and the red lamp illuminates for 5 seconds.
Speaker checkThe buzzer sounds twice, and the passenger's side door speaker* beeps.
Microphone checkThe buzzer sounds three times, and your voice is played though the microphone to the passenger's side door speaker*.
Test reportChecks the telephone line.
note*: When diagnosis code B1856 and B1864 are set, the emergency call speaker will respond instead.
