A/T INITIAL LEARNING PROCEDURE
2020 / L200/L200 SPORTERO / EU - Workshop Manual
23 -AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION > 23C-AUTOMATIC TRANSMISSION<R6AWH, V6AWH> > TROUBLESHOOTING <A/T> > A/T INITIAL LEARNING PROCEDURE
caution
  • When the A/T or A/T-ECU is replaced, always initialise the learned value, and perform the initial learning.
  • Do not raise the A/T fluid temperature with engine stall.
  • If the A/T fluid temperature is not between 65 and 110°C, the initial learning cannot be performed.
1. Raise the A/T fluid temperature by leaving the vehicle at idle or performing a city driving. Subsequently, check the A/T fluid temperature data using the M.U.T.-III, and check that the A/T fluid temperature is at between 65 and 110°C.
noteAfter the A/T fluid temperature is raised, check the garage shift shock and shift shock status before the learning.
2. Make the A/T-ECU learn about the garage shift shock according to the procedure below:
(1) Depress the brake pedal with the vehicle stationary.
(2) Move and hold the selector lever to the "N" position for 3 seconds, and then move and hold it to the "D" position for 3 seconds.
(3) Repeat step (2) 5 times.
3. Make the A/T-ECU learn about the shift shock according to the procedure below:
(1) Accelerate the vehicle with the selector lever in "D" position until the speed reaches approximately 110 km/h and the 6th gear is engaged (throttle opening amount: approximately 25% to 35%).
(2) Subsequently, release the accelerator pedal to decelerate, and bring the vehicle to stop within 60 seconds.
(3) Repeat steps (1) and (2) 10 times.
4. Check that the garage shift shock and shift shock are alleviated compared to these before the learning.
