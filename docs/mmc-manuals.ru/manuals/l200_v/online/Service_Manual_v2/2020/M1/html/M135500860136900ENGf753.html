Inspection Procedure 12: ASTC-ECU Power Supply Circuit System.
 
 
caution
  • Whenever ECU is replaced, ensure that the CAN bus lines are normal.
  • When the hydraulic unit (integrated with ASTC-ECU) is replaced, always carry out the calibration of the master cylinder pressure sensor, steering wheel sensor and the G and yaw rate sensor (Refer to ).
 
 
OPERATION
  • The ASTC-ECU contains the power supply circuit (+BV terminal) for the solenoid valve. The solenoid valve is energized by the valve relay, which is incorporated in the ASTC-ECU.
  • The ASTC-ECU contains the power supply circuit (+BM terminal) for the pump motor. The pump motor is energized by the motor relay, which is incorporated in the ASTC-ECU.
  • The ASTC-ECU contains the power supply circuit (IG1 terminal) for itself. When the ignition switch (IG1) is turned ON, the voltage is applied to the relay incorporated in the ETACS-ECU to turn ON the relay, and the power is supplied from the fusible link through the multi-purpose fuse.
  • When malfunction occurs in ASTC-ECU power supply, the communication with M.U.T.-III becomes unavailable.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Battery failure
  • Improper tightening of battery terminal
  • Charging system failed
  • Damaged harness wires and connectors
  • Fuse and fusible link malfunction
  • Improper tightening of earth bolt
  • Malfunction of the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU)
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. Check the batter.
Refer to GROUP 54A - Battery Test .
Is the battery in good condition?
Go to Step 3.
Charge or replace the battery. Then go to Step 2.
 
STEP 2. Check the charging system.
Refer to GROUP 16 - Charging System .
Is the charging system in good condition?
Go to Step 3.
Repair or replace the charging system component(s).
 
STEP 3. Measure the resistance at the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(1) Connector check: the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU), the fuses (relay box) and the fusible links.
(2) Disconnect the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(3) Measure the resistance between +BV terminal and body earth.
OK: No continuity
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 4.
The power supply circuit may be short. Repair the wiring harness wires or connectors between the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector +BV terminal and the fuse (relay box), the fuses (relay box) or the fusible links. Then go to Step 11.
 
STEP 4. Measure the resistance at the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(1) Connector check: the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector and the fusible links
(2) Disconnect the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector and remove the fusible links.
(3) Measure the resistance between +BM terminal and body earth.
OK: No continuity
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 5.
The power supply circuit may be short. Repair the wiring harness wires or connectors between the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector +BM terminal and the fusible links, or the fusible links. Then go to Step 11.
 
STEP 5. Measure the resistance at the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(1) Connector check: the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector and the junction block
(2) Disconnect the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(3) Measure the resistance between the IG1 terminal and body earth.
OK: No continuity
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 6.
The power supply circuit may be short. Repair the wiring harness wires or connectors between the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector IG1 terminal and the junction block. Then go to Step 11.
 
STEP 6. Measure the voltage at the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(1) Disconnect the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(2) Measure the voltage between +BV terminal and body earth.
OK: Battery voltage
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 7.
The power supply circuit may be open. Repair the wiring harness wires or connectors between the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector +BV terminal and the fuse (relay box) or the fuses (relay box). Then go to Step 11.
 
STEP 7. Measure the voltage at the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(1) Disconnect the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(2) Measure the voltage between +BM terminal and body earth.
OK: Battery voltage
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 8.
The power supply circuit may be open. Repair the wiring harness wires or connectors between the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector +BM terminal and the fusible links, or the fusible links. Then go to Step 11.
 
STEP 8. Measure the voltage at the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(1) Disconnect the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(2) Ignition switch: "ON"
(3) Measure the voltage between the IG1 terminal and body earth.
OK: Battery voltage
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 9.
The power supply circuit may be open. Repair the wiring harness wires or connectors between the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector IG1 terminal and the junction block. Then go to Step 11.
 
STEP 9. Measure the resistance at the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(1) Disconnect the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector.
(2) Measure the resistance between the GND1 terminal and body earth as well as between the GND2 terminal and body earth.
OK: Continuity exists (2 Ω or less).
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 10.
The earth circuit may be open. Repair the wiring harness wires or connectors between the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) connector GND1 terminal and the fusible links as well as between the GND2 terminal and the fusible links. Then go to Step 11.
 
STEP 10. Retest the system.
Make sure that the M.U.T.-III cable is properly connected and the V.C.I. switch is ON.
Is the communication with M.U.T.-III possible?
The trouble can be an intermittent malfunction (Refer to GROUP 00 - How to Cope with Intermittent Malfunction ).
Replace the hydraulic unit assembly (integrated with ASTC-ECU) (Refer to ). Then go to Step 11.
 
STEP 11. Retest the system.
Make sure that the M.U.T.-III cable is properly connected and the V.C.I. switch is ON.
Is the communication with M.U.T.-III possible?
Return to Step 1.
This diagnosis is complete.
