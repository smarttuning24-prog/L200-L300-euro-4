Inspection Procedure 1: Engine does not start
 
 
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
If the engine does not start when the selector lever is in the "P" or "N" position, the cause is probably a malfunction of engine system, inhibitor switch system, transmission control cable.
noteAs a cause of the trouble, battery voltage abnormality is suspected. However, in this item, it shall be presupposed that there is no abnormality to the above.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of the engine system
  • Malfunction of the inhibitor switch system
  • Malfunction of the transmission control cable
 
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. Engine system check
Refer to GROUP 13A - Troubleshooting .
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 2.
Repair the engine system.

  • Item 33: Inhibitor switch: R
  • Item 34: Inhibitor switch: N
  • Item 35: Inhibitor switch: D
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 4.
Go to Step 3.

Refer to .
