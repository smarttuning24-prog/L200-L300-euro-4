REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
 
 
cautionWhen the EGR cooler is replaced, use the M.U.T.-III to initialise the learning value (Refer to GROUP 00 - Precautions Before Service, Initialisation Procedure for Learning Value in Common Rail Engine ).
Pre-removal Operation
  • Under Skid Plate and Engine Room Under Cover Front A and B Removal (Refer to GROUP 51 - Under Cover ).
  • Engine Coolant Draining (Refer to GROUP 14 - On-vehicle Service, Engine Coolant Replacement ).
Post-installation Operation
  • Engine Coolant Refilling (Refer to GROUP 14 - On-vehicle Service, Engine Coolant Replacement ).
  • Under Skid Plate and Engine Room Under Cover Front A and B Installation (Refer to GROUP 51 - Under Cover ).

1.Vacuum hose connection
2.EGR valve connector

4.Water hose D

REMOVAL SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> EGR VALVE PIPE B/EGR VALVE ASSEMBLY REMOVAL
When the EGR pipe B and EGR valve assembly are removed, must prevent the condensate water, oil, coolant water is not applied to the EGR motor by using waste cloth.
 
 
 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS
 
 
>>A<< EGR VALVE ASSEMBLY/EGR VALVE PIPE GASKET/EGR VALVE PIPE A INSTALLATION
1. Install each gasket with its projection being positioned as shown in the figure.
2. Temporarily tighten the EGR valve assembly mounting bolts.
Temporary tightening torque: 1.3 ± 0.7 N·m
3. Temporarily tighten EGR valve pipe A mounting bolts.
Temporary tightening torque: 1.3 ± 0.7 N·m
4. Tighten each mounting bolts to the specified torque in the order as shown in the figure.
Tightening torque: 20 ± 2 N·m
 
 
 
>>B<< EGR VALVE PIPE GASKET/EGR VALVE PIPE B INSTALLATION
1. Install each gasket with its projection being positioned as shown in the figure.
2. Temporarily tighten the inlet manifold side mounting bolts.
Temporary tightening torque: 1.3 ± 0.7 N·m
3. Temporarily tighten the EGR valve side mounting bolts.
Temporary tightening torque: 1.3 ± 0.7 N·m
4. Tighten the inlet manifold side mounting bolts to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 20 ± 2 N·m
5. Tighten the EGR valve side mounting bolts to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 20 ± 2 N·m
