DISASSEMBLY
 
 
1. BRAKE PISTON NO. 2 REMOVAL
1.
cautionBe careful not to damage the brake piston No. 2.
While holding the brake piston No. 2 by hand, apply compressed air (300 kPa) into the oil passage of the brake cylinder No. 2 as shown in the illustration and remove the brake piston No. 2.
2. Remove the 2 O-rings from the brake piston No. 2.
 

 
 
2. BRAKE PISTON NO. 1 REMOVAL
1.
cautionBe careful not to damage the brake piston No. 1.
While holding the brake piston No. 1 by hand, apply compressed air (300 kPa) into the oil passage of the brake cylinder No. 1 as shown in the illustration and remove the brake piston No. 1.
2. Remove the 2 O-rings from the brake piston No. 1.
 
 
 
 
REASSEMBLY
 
 
INSPECTION BEFORE REASSEMBLY
Refer to Second Brake Inspection .
  • Inspect Brake Disc Set No. 1 (B1)
  • Check Brake Piston Return Spring Sub-assembly No. 1 (B1)
  • Inspect Brake Disc Set No. 2 (B2)
  • Check Brake Piston Return Spring Sub-assembly No. 2 (B2)
  • Check Pack Clearance of Brake Disc No. 2 (B2)
  • Check Pack Clearance of Brake Disc No. 1 (B1)

 
 
1. BRAKE PISTON NO. 1 INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to 2 new O-rings and the brake piston No. 1.
2. Install them to the brake piston No. 1.
O-ring size:
 
Inner diameter (mm)Thickness (mm)
119.62.62
148.92.62
3. With the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 1 installed to the brake piston No. 1, align the 2 claws of the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 1 with the 2 claws of the brake cylinder No. 1, and then install the brake piston No. 1 to the brake cylinder No. 1.
 

 
 
2. BRAKE PISTON NO. 2 INSTALLATION
1. Apply ATF to 2 new O-rings and the brake piston No. 2.
2. Install them to the brake piston No. 2.
O-ring size:
 
Inner diameter (mm)Thickness (mm)
147.02.62
170.22.62
3. With the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 2 installed to the brake piston No. 2, align the claw of the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 2 with the claw of the brake cylinder No. 2, and then install the brake piston No. 2 to the brake cylinder No. 2.
