REMOVAL SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY DISCONNECTION
caution
  • Suspend the propeller shaft from the body with wire, etc. to prevent it from falling.
  • Do not drop, shock, or scratch the propeller shaft.
Make mating marks on the front propeller shaft flange yoke and the differential carrier companion flange. Disconnect the front propeller shaft.
 
 
 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS
 
 
>>A<< VACUUM HOSE INSTALLATION <VEHICLES WITH FREEWHEEL CLUTCH>
Install the vacuum hoses so that they match the identification colours of the actuator assembly nipple.
 

 
 
>>B<< FRONT PROPELLER SHAFT ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
cautionTighten the installation bolts and nuts after removing oil and grease from threads to prevent them from loosening due to lubrication.
Install the front propeller shaft so that the mating marks of the flange yoke and the differential carrier companion flange are aligned.
