REMOVAL SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> OIL PIPE REMOVAL
cautionTake care not to allow foreign objects to get into the oil passage hole of the turbocharger assembly after the oil pipe is removed.
 
 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS
 
 
>>A<< STUD INSTALLATION
The stud should protrude 24 to 26 mm from the end of the turbocharger.
 
 
 
>>B<< STUD INSTALLATION
The stud should protrude 26 to 28 mm from the end of the turbocharger.
 
 
 
>>C<< TURBOCHARGER ASSEMBLY INSTALLATION
1. Check the internal surface, the eye bolt and the mating surface of the oil pipe and water pipe for clogging, and clean if necessary.
2.
cautionBe careful not to allow foreign material to enter the turbocharger.
If deposits of carbon are accumulated on the turbocharger oil passage, remove them and clean using the compressed air.
3. Add clean engine oil through the oil feed pipe port on the turbocharger.
 
 
>>D<< AIR INLET FITTING GASKET INSTALLATION
Install the air inlet fitting gasket as its projection is in the direction shown.
 
 
 
>>E<< WATER PIPE GASKET INSTALLATION
The tab on the water pipe gasket should be position as shown.
 
 
 
>>F<< TURBOCHARGER GASKET INSTALLATION
Install the turbocharger gasket as its projection is in the direction shown.
 
 
 
>>G<< OIL RETURN PIPE GASKET INSTALLATION
The tab on the oil return pipe gasket should be position as shown.
 
 
 
>>H<< OIL PIPE INSTALLATION
1. Tighten the eye bolt by hand.
2. Tighten the oil pipe clamp mounting bolts by hand.
3. Tighten the eye bolt to the specified torque, being careful not to deform the oil pipe.
Tightening torque: 17 ± 2 N·m
4. Tighten the oil pipe clamp mounting bolt to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 28 ± 1 N·m
