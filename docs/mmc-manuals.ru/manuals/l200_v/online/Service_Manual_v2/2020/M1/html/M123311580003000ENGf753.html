1. INSPECT CENTRE PLANETARY GEAR ASSEMBLY
cautionIf the clearance is not as specified, replace the centre planetary gear assembly.
Using a feeler gauge, measure the centre planetary gear pinion thrust clearance.
Standard value: 0.12 - 0.68 mm
 

 
 
2. INSPECT BRAKE DISC SET NO. 2 (B2)
Check the lining of all discs.
note
  • If the lining is flaking or has changed color, or if it is worn or the print mark is wearing away, replace with a new disc. When replacing, check the contact surfaces between the flange and plate and the disc. If they are scratched or have changed color, replace with new parts.
  • Before replacing with new discs, soak them at least 2 hours in ATF.
 

 
 
3. CHECK PACK CLEARANCE OF BRAKE DISC SET NO. 2 (B2)
1. Install the snap ring to the transmission case.
2.
caution
  • Be careful not to expand the snap ring too much.
  • Measure the circumference of the length A at 3 places, and make sure that the average value is within the specified range.
With the snap ring pulled up on the converter housing side, measure as shown in the illustration using vernier calipers.
Length A: 59.72 - 59.88 mm
3. Make sure that the brake piston No. 2 is pushed fully into the brake cylinder No. 2.
4.
caution
  • Measure the circumference of the brake cylinder No. 2 with the brake piston No. 2 at 3 places, and make sure that the average value is within the specified range.
  • If the average value is outside the specified range, reinstall the brake cylinder No. 2 with the brake piston No. 2.
Using vernier calipers, measure as shown in the illustration.
Length B: 28.22 - 28.54 mm
5. Stack the 2 flanges, the 5 discs, and the 4 plates on top of the surface plate as shown in the illustration.
note
  • Check the number and order of flanges, discs and plates.
  • Use a flange of assembling plate.
  • When using new disc and plate, please use flange of 2.4 mm thickness.
6.
cautionMeasure the circumference of the flange at 3 places, and make sure that the average value is within the specified range.
With the flange pressed against the surface plate, measure as shown in the illustration using vernier calipers.
Length C: 26.06 - 26.71 mm
noteMake sure that the measurement load is 3.92 N - 4.9 N.
7.
cautionIf the B2 brake pack clearance is not as specified, select an appropriate brake flange No. 2.
Calculate the pack clearance from the measured value, the disc distortion compensation value (0.37 mm), and the snap ring thickness (4.0 mm).
Standard value: 0.50 - 0.80 mm
(B2 brake pack clearance = A - B - C - 0.37 - 4.0)

4. CHECK BRAKE PISTON RETURN SPRING SUB-ASSEMBLY NO. 2
cautionIf the free length is smaller than the minimum, replace the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 2 with a new one.
Using vernier calipers, measure the free length of the brake piston return spring sub-assembly No. 2.
Standard value: 16.68 mm
 

 
 
5. INSPECT FR PLANETARY GEAR ASSEMBLY
1.
cautionIf the clearance is not as specified, replace the FR planetary gear assembly.
Using a feeler gauge, measure the FR planetary gear pinion thrust clearance.
Standard value: 0.2 - 0.6 mm
2.
caution
  • Measure the inside diameter at several places and take an average. (More than 3 places) If the average is greater than the maximum, replace the FR planetary gear assembly with a new one.
  • When the FR planetary gear assembly is replaced, inspect the surface in contact with the clutch hub sub-assembly. If the surface is scratched or discolored, replace the clutch hub sub-assembly with a new one.
Using a dial indicator, measure the inside diameter of the FR planetary gear assembly bushing.
Standard value: 35.811 - 35.835 mm
 

 
 
6. INSPECT SUN GEAR INPUT DRUM
caution
  • Measure the inside diameter at several places and take an average. (More than 3 places) If the average is greater than the maximum, replace the sun gear input drum with a new one.
  • When the sun gear input drum is replaced, inspect the surface in contact with the clutch hub sub-assembly. If the surface is scratched or discolored, replace the clutch hub sub-assembly with a new one.
Using a dial indicator, measure the inside diameter of the sun gear input drum bushing.
Standard value: 35.811 - 35.835 mm
 

 
 
7. CHECK CLUTCH HUB SUB-ASSEMBLY BUSHING
caution
  • Measure the inside diameter at several places and take an average. (More than 3 places) If the average is greater than the maximum, replace the clutch hub sub-assembly with a new one.
  • When the clutch hub sub-assembly is replaced, inspect the surface in contact the intermediate shaft. If the surface is scratched or discolored, replace the intermediate shaft with a new one.
Using a dial indicator, measure the inside diameter of the clutch hub sub-assembly bushing.
Standard value: 26.037 - 26.062 mm
