DOOR FIT ADJUSTMENT
2020 / L200/L200 SPORTERO / EU - Workshop Manual
42 -BODY > 42A-BODY > DOOR > ON-VEHICLE SERVICE > DOOR FIT ADJUSTMENT
1.
caution
  • Attach protection tapes to the fender and door edges where the hinge is installed.
  • Do not rotate special tool door hinge adjusting wrench MB991163 with a torque of over 98 N·m.
  • Remove the front fender rear splash shield (Refer to ).
When the clearance between the front door and the body is uneven:
(1) Apply protective tapes to the fender and front door edge around the hinge mounting area.
(2) Remove the front pillar pad (Refer to ).
(3) Use the special tool door hinge adjusting wrench (MB990834 or MB991163) to loosen the body-side door hinge mounting bolts.
(4) Move the front door to adjust until the clearance around the front door is even.
2. When the clearance between the rear door and the body is uneven <Club cab/Double cab>:
(1) Remove the centre pillar lower trim <Double cab> or the rear pillar lower trim <Club cab> (Refer to GROUP 52A, Interior Trim ).
(2) Apply protective tapes to the body and rear door edge around the hinge mounting area.
(3) Loosen the body-side door hinge mounting nuts and bolts.
(4) Move the rear door to adjust until the clearance around the rear door is even.
3. When the door is not flush with the vehicle body surface:
(1) Loosen the door-side hinge mounting bolts.
(2) Move the door to adjust so that the door is flush with the body surface.
(3) If adjustment is not possible, replace the door-side door hinge mounting bolt with the adjustment bolt (MU001031).
noteIf the adjustment bolt has been already installed, reuse it.
(4) Move the door to adjust so that the door is flush with the body surface.
(5) Tighten the door-side door hinge mounting bolt to the specified torque.
Tightening torque: 27 ± 6 N·m
4. When the door is stiff to close and open:
(1)
cautionTo avoid inadvertent airbag deployment, always turn ignition switch to the "OFF" position and wait 60 seconds or more before adjust the striker.
Adjustment using the striker (toward the inside of the vehicle and vertical direction):
Install a temporary bolt instead of the striker mounting bolt, and use the remover bar (Special tool: MB990939) and a hammer to tap the temporary bolt to the desired direction.
(2) Adjustment by using the striker (toward the outside of the vehicle)
Use special tools, sliding hammer (MB990211) and body puller (MB990243) to pull the striker toward the outside of the vehicle.
On Club cab model, adjust the rear door latch strikers (upper and lower).
(3) Adjustment by using shims (forward and rearward)
Increase or decrease the number of shims so that the striker engages with the door latch properly.
On Club cab model, adjust the rear door upper latch assembly.
