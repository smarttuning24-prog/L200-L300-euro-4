REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION
 
 
caution
  • When removing or installing the glow plug, be careful not to let the glow plug end touch the cylinder head hole.
  • Do not reuse the glow plug that has been dropped.
  • Do not use power tools for glow plug installation otherwise the glow plug might be damaged.
  • Manually tighten the glow plug until it is seated. Then, tighten it to the specified torque.
Pre-removal and Post-installation Operation
  • Harness Bracket Assembly Removal and Installation (Refer to GROUP 13A - Common Rail <4N14> ). <4N14>
  • Harness Bracket Assembly Removal and Installation (Refer to GROUP 13A - Common Rail <4N15> ). <4N15>

REMOVAL SERVICE POINT
 
 
<<A>> GLOW PLUG HARNESS REMOVAL
1.
cautionDo not pull out the connector aslant. Pulling out the connector aslant may cause energisation failure of the connector.
Pull out the glow plug connector straight to the direction of the glow plug axis.
2. Remove the clips to remove the glow plug harness.
 
 
 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINT
 
 
>>A<< GLOW PLUG HARNESS INSTALLATION
1. Fix the glow plug harness to the engine with clips.
2.
cautionDo not insert the connector aslant. Inserting the connector aslant may cause energisation failure of the connector.
Temporarily install the glow plug connector by inserting it straight to the direction of the glow plug axis.
3. Make sure that the glow plug connector is inserted straight to the glow plug axis, and press the connector until it clicks.
