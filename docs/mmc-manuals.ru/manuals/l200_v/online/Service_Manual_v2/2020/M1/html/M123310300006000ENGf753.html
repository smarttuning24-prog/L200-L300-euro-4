1. INSPECT FR PLANETARY RING GEAR FLANGE SUB-ASSEMBLY
caution
  • Measure the inside diameter at several places and take an average. (More than 3 places) If the average is greater than the maximum, replace the FR planetary ring gear flange sub-assembly with a new one.
  • When the FR planetary ring gear flange sub-assembly is replaced, inspect the surface in contact with the FR planetary gear assembly. If the surface is scratched or discolored, replace the FR planetary gear assembly with a new one.
Using a dial indicator, measure the inside diameter of the FR planetary ring gear flange sub-assembly bushing.
Standard value: 54.038 - 54.063 mm
 

 
 
2. INSPECT RR PLANETARY RING GEAR FLANGE
caution
  • Measure the inside diameter at several places and take an average. (More than 3 places) If the average is greater than the maximum, replace the RR planetary ring gear flange with a new one.
  • When the RR planetary ring gear flange is replaced, inspect the surface in contact with the intermediate shaft. If the surface is scratched or discolored, replace the intermediate shaft with a new one.
Using a dial indicator, measure the inside diameter of the RR planetary ring gear flange bushing.
Standard value: 32.176 - 32.201 mm
