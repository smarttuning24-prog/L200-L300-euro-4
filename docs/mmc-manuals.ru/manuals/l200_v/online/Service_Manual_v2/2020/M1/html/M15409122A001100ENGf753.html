CHANGING THE COLOUR OF VEHICLE ICON
When you replace the camera-ECU, the colour of vehicle icon displayed in the bird's eye-view is initialized. Change the colour of vehicle icon to the colour set before the replacement by following the procedures below.
 
 
 
 
How to change the colour of vehicle icon <Using the camera switch>
1. Press the camera switch to display the bird’s eye-view/front-view.
2. Press and hold the camera switch for at least 1.5 seconds to enter the colour selection mode.
3. Press the camera switch until the desired colour appears on the display.
note
  • Each time you press the camera switch, the colour of vehicle icon in the bird’s eye-view will change to the next one.
  • When the following conditions are met, the colour of vehicle icon does not change and the colour selection mode is canceled.
    • Shift lever is moved to the reverse position. <M/T>
    • Selector lever is moved to the "R" position. <A/T>
    • You do not operate anything for 30 seconds.
    • Ignition switch is turned to the "LOCK" (OFF) position.
4. When the colour of vehicle icon is changed to the desired colour, press and hold the camera switch for a few seconds to exit the colour selection mode.
 
 
 
How to change the colour of vehicle icon <Using M.U.T.-III>
Using the customisation function of multi around monitor in M.U.T.-III, the colour of vehicle icon can be changed. The customisation information is held even when the battery is disconnected.
