Inspection Procedure 5: Slip at backward driving
 
 
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
If a slip occurs during backward driving, the cause is probably an abnormal line pressure or a malfunction of the engine system, torque converter, valve body assembly, or oil pump.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of the engine system
  • Insufficient A/T fluid amount, immixing of water or unspecified A/T fluid
  • Malfunction of the torque converter
  • Malfunction of the valve body assembly [SL solenoid, clutch control solenoid (SL4)]
  • Malfunction of the A/T-ECU
  • Malfunction of the transmission assembly [oil pump, brake No. 1 (B1) or No. 4 (B4), etc.]
 
 
 
DIAGNOSIS PROCEDURE
STEP 1. A/T fluid check
Check the A/T fluid for amount and condition (not severely contaminated), and fill or replace the A/T fluid if necessary. Then, check the trouble symptom again.
Is the trouble symptom reproduced?
Go to Step 2.
The procedure is complete.
 
STEP 2. Hydraulic pressure test
Measure the line pressure (Refer to ).
noteIf a slip occurs during backward driving, the line pressure may be lower than the standard value.
Is the check result normal?
Go to Step 3.
Go to Step 4.
 
STEP 3. Torque converter stall test
Carry out the torque converter stall test (Refer to ), and replace the torque converter if the result is out of the standard value. Then, check the trouble symptom again.
Is the trouble symptom reproduced?
Go to Step 5.
The procedure is complete.
 
STEP 4. Valve body assembly replacement
If the trouble symptom is not solved, replace the valve body assembly. Then, check the trouble symptom again.
Is the trouble symptom reproduced?
Go to Step 5.
The procedure is complete.
 
STEP 5. A/T-ECU replacement
If the trouble symptom is not solved, replace the A/T-ECU. Then, check the trouble symptom again.
Is the trouble symptom reproduced?
After checking the oil pump for damage, check each clutch and brake for any damage, and replace if necessary. [It is highly possible that the brake No. 1 (B1) or No. 4 (B4) that engage in reverse gear are damaged.]
The procedure is complete.
