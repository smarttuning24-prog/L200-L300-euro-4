CLUTCH INTERLOCK SWITCH CONTINUITY CHECK
cautionDo not apply grease or lubricant to the switch and the switch installation section to avoid malfunction of the switch. In addition, do not use gloves which have grease on them.
1. Check the clutch pedal.
2. Connect an ohmmeter to the clutch interlock switch connector, and then check for continuity when the clutch pedal is fully depressed and when it is released outward.
 
Tester connectionPedal positionSpecified condition
1-2Fully depressedContinuity (Less than 2 ohms)
ReleasedOpen circuit
3. If the continuity is incorrect, replace the clutch interlock switch (Refer to ).
