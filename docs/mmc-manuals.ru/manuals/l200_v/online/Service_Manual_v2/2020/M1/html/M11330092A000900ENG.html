REMOVAL AND INSTALLATION <4N14>
 
 
caution
  • When the engine-ECU is replaced, another ECU related to KOS system should not replaced together. Always replace the ECU by ones when the multiple ECU is replaced, and then replace the next ECU after registering the necessary IDs by using M.U.T.-III.
  • When the engine-ECU is replaced, coding must be performed.
  • When the engine-ECU is replaced, using M.U.T.-III to write the chassis number (Refer to GROUP 00 - Precautions before Service, How to Perform Chassis Number Writing).
  • When the engine-ECU is replaced, register the injector identification code and perform the small injection quantity learning (Refer to GROUP 00 - Precautions Before Service, What The Common Rail Engine Learns).

REMOVAL SERVICE POINTS
 
 
<<A>> BREAK-OFF BOLT REMOVAL
1. <When using reverse screw tap>
(1) Use a drill to make a hole just deeply enough for the tap to stand on the break-off.
(2) Remove the break-off with a reverse screw tap.
2. <When using the flat-tipped screwdriver>
(1) Apply force towards the direction to loose the break-off bolt by a flat-tipped screwdriver.
(2) Remove the break-off bolt.
 

 
 
<<B>> ENGINE-ECU CONNECTOR DISCONNECTION
cautionBe sure to press the connector cover when turning the lock lever, or the connector may be damaged.
noteIf the connector cover should come off, engage the lock lever gear with the connector gear. Then, install the connector cover to the connector. Press the lock button to unlock the lock lever. While pressing the connector cover against the connector, turn the lock lever to the vertical position. Then, disconnect the connector from the engine-ECU.
Press the lock button to unlock the lock lever. While pressing the connector cover against the engine-ECU, turn the lock lever to the vertical position. Then, disconnect the connector from the engine-ECU.
 
 
 
 
INSTALLATION SERVICE POINTS
 
 
>>A<< ENGINE-ECU CONNECTOR CONNECTION
1.
cautionWhen inserting the connector to the engine-ECU, be careful not to apply force in the directions other than the inserting direction.
Move the lock lever of the connector to the unlock status, and then install the connector to the engine-ECU. Insert the connector as far as it goes.
2.
cautionMake sure that the connector is inserted sufficiently before locking it, or the lock lever may be damaged.
While pressing the connector toward the engine-ECU side, turn the lock lever to lock the connector.
 
 
 
>>B<< BREAK-OFF BOLT INSTALLATION
Tighten the break-off bolt until its head is broken off.
 
 
>>C<< INITIALISATION OPERATION
Turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position and then to "LOCK" (OFF) position and hold it for at least 10 seconds.
