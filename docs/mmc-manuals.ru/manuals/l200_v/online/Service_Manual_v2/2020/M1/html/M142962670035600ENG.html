Inspection Procedure 15: Electric steering lock does not become locked (At power supply mode "OFF").
 
 
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
The electric steering lock operates in accordance with a door switch signal, the P range detection switch signal, keyless entry function lock operation or door entry function lock operation. When the electric steering lock is not activated, a diagnosis code may be set in the KOS&OSS-ECU, the driver's door switch circuit or the electric steering lock may be defective.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of KOS&OSS-ECU
  • Malfunction of the door switch
  • Electric steering lock malfunction
  • Malfunction of selector lever assembly (ON seizure of P range detection switch)
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
