AdBlue™ REFILL
2020 / L200/L200 SPORTERO / EU - Workshop Manual
17 -ENGINE AND EMISSION CONTROL > EMISSION CONTROL <DIESEL> > UREA SCR SYSTEM<4N14> > AdBlue REFILL
caution
  • Do not refill the urea tank with diesel exhaust fluid other than AdBlue™.
  • If any AdBlue™ gets on painted surfaces, wash it off immediately with clean water.
  • If the AdBlue™ is adhered to the vehicle, wipe the area with a wet cloth.
 
 
<When replenishing by confirming the maximum refill quantity by the M.U.T.-III>
1.
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Connect the M.U.T.-III to the diagnosis connector.
2. Turn the ignition switch to "ON" position (But do not start the engine).
3. Check "Item No. 64: Urea tank remaining volume" from the M.U.T.-III data list.
4. Confirm the maximum refill quantity in the table below according to the value read out in Step 3.

note
  • *1: This value will not be 100% even if the urea tank is full, because the upper limit for the reading of the urea tank level sensor is set to 100%.
  • *2: The maximum refill quantity can also be confirmed according to the value of M.U.T.-III data list item No. 8: Urea tank level.
  • *3: This is only for reference because the urea consumption amount varies depending on the driving conditions.
  • *4: It is no problem if the refill quantity is less than this value because this is the maximum refill quantity.
5. After turning the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position, remove the M.U.T.-III.
6. Replenish AdBlue™ via the AdBlue™ refill port by considering the maximum refill quantity which you confirmed in Step 4.
note
  • Only when the AdBlue™ remaining amount warning is displayed on the combination meter, proceed to Step 7.
  • When rechecking the maximum refill quantity after replenishing AdBlue™, be sure to perform Step 7 through Step 12 before the recheck.
7. Connect the M.U.T.-III to the diagnosis connector.
8. Turn the ignition switch to "ON" position (But do not start the engine).
9. Select "DCU" from System Select Screen of the M.U.T.-III.
10. From DCU Screen, select "Special Function".
11. From Special Function Screen, select "Reset".
12. Carry out "Item No. 1: Urea tank level" from Reset Screen of the M.U.T.-III.
13. Check that the AdBlue™ remaining amount warning has disappeared from the combination meter.
noteIf the refill quantity at one time is 3 litres or more, the AdBlue™ remaining amount warning can be turned off without performing the urea tank level reset. The warning can be turned off by performing the following procedure. Stop the vehicle on a level surface, and turn the ignition switch from "LOCK" (OFF) to "ON" (do not start the engine). Then, leave the vehicle for approximately 5 minutes or more.
14. After turning the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position, remove the M.U.T.-III.

3. Replenish AdBlue™ via the AdBlue™ refill port by considering the maximum refill quantity which you confirmed in Step 2.
4. Turn the ignition switch to the "ON" position, and leave the vehicle for approximately 5 minutes.
5. Check that the AdBlue™ remaining amount warning has disappeared from the combination meter.
6. If the AdBlue™ remaining amount warning does not go out, turn off the AdBlue™ remaining amount warning by the following procedures.
(1)
cautionTo prevent damage to the M.U.T.-III, always turn the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position before connecting or disconnecting the M.U.T.-III.
Connect the M.U.T.-III to the diagnosis connector.
(2) Turn the ignition switch to "ON" position (But do not start the engine).
(3) Select "DCU" from System Select Screen of the M.U.T.-III.
(4) From DCU Screen, select "Special Function".
(5) From Special Function Screen, select "Reset".
(6) Carry out "Item No. 1: Urea tank level" from Reset Screen of the M.U.T.-III.
(7) Check that the AdBlue™ remaining amount warning has disappeared from the combination meter.
noteIf the refill quantity at one time is 3 litres or more, the AdBlue™ remaining amount warning can be turned off without performing the urea tank level reset. The warning can be turned off by performing the following procedure. Stop the vehicle on a level surface, and turn the ignition switch from "LOCK" (OFF) to "ON" (do not start the engine). Then, leave the vehicle for approximately 5 minutes or more.
(8) After turning the ignition switch to the "LOCK" (OFF) position, remove the M.U.T.-III.
