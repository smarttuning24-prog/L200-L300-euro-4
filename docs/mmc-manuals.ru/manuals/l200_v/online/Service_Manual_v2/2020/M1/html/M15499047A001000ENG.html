Inspection Procedure 3: The system is not turned on and off by operating the blind spot warning switch.
 
 
cautionBefore replacing the ECU, ensure that the power supply circuit, the earth circuit and the communication circuit are normal.
 
 
COMMENTS ON TROUBLE SYMPTOM
If the system is not turned on and off by operating the blind spot warning switch, a circuit (among the gateway-ECU (for BSW), the blind spot warning lamp and earth), the gateway-ECU (for BSW), or the BSW-ECU (master) may be defective.
notePrior to diagnosis, check the power supply circuit to the gateway-ECU (for BSW). If no faults are detected, follow this troubleshooting procedure.
 
 
PROBABLE CAUSES
  • Malfunction of the blind spot warning switch
  • Malfunction of the gateway-ECU (for BSW)
  • Malfunction of the BSW-ECU (master)
  • Damaged wiring harness and connectors.
